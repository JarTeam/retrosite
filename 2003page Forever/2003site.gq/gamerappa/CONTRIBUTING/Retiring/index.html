If you're tried on managing your 2003site subsite. Here's how to retire it.

Retiring a sub-subsite/subpage just requires deleting the folder/file and remove any references to it.

 

Step 1. Add a message on your index that explains on why you're moving on from 2003site. It should include the date that the entire subsite (i mean everything in it) will be wiped out.

Step 2. Once the date reaches and you wiped your subsite out. Copy TEMPLATE.html to your subsite and change the name to Index.html

Step 3. Request to support@cloudsoftware.cf that you want to no longer be a outside collabrator of Rappa Sites.

Step 4. After roughly 2 weeks, another 2003site collabrator will delete your subsite.
