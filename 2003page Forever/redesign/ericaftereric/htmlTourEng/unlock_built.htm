Built-in Photo Sharing and Organization

With Windows XP, viewing photos and sharing them with friends, family, and colleagues couldn't be easier. Just plug in your digital camera and your photos appear instantly—ready for you to edit, e-mail, or print. You can even publish images directly to a Web site or save them on CD. Or send them to an online retailer for professional quality prints.
