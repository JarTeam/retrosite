If you're tried on managing your 2003page subsite. Here's how to retire it.

Retiring a sub-subsite/subpage just requires deleting the folder/file and remove any references to it.

 

Step 1. Add a message on your index that explains on why you're moving on from 2003page. It should include the date that the entire subsite (i mean everything in it) will be wiped out.

Step 2. Once the date reaches and you wiped your subsite out. Copy TEMPLATE.html to your subsite.

Step 3. Request to support@cloudsoftware.cf that you want to no longer be a outside collabrator of XPorg.

Step 4. After roughly 2 weeks, a remaining 2003page collabrator will delete your subsite.
