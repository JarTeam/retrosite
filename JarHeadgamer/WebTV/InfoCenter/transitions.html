Transitions

 

If you want to give your website a little something extra, transitions are the way to go.

 

Transitions are actually very simple. Just pop the <transition> attribute in your <body> tag with the transition type you want and you'll be good to go. It's worth mentioning that trying to use a transition in an E-Mail or Newsgroup post won't work.

You can either use a slide transition or a wipe transition, in any direction. I won't go in depth on what they all do because the names are pretty self explanatory. You can choose from the following 12 transitions:


slideup
slidedown
slideleft
slideright

wipeup
wipedown
wipeleft
wiperight
wipelefttop
wipeleftbottom
wiperighttop
wiperightbottom  

Enjoy!
