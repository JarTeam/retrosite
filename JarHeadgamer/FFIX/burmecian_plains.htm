THE MURKY PLAINS

After Zidane's group leaves Gizamaluke's Grotto, head for the Burmecian capital to the north. However, there are a few enemies to fight along the way, and Quina could stand to learn a few more abilities by eating its way through them.

To the east of the Grotto is the North Gate. When you investigate here, Freya will go through a range of emotions while Quina looks for good food. There is also a Hi-Potion and a Tent to collect near the gates.

There's a whirling sandstorm to the southwest of Burmecia, but you can't enter this area right now. However, beyond the desert and through a short valley is the Healing Shore, where you can find Chocobo tracks. If you found the Healing Shore Chocograph in the Hot and Cold game, this is where you receive the Blue Chocobo that can swim in water. You can backtrack to the forest if you're desperate, or you can always come back and get the Blue Chocobo later.
