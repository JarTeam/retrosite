HIGH ABOVE THE MIST

The Ice Caverns exit to an expansive view of a high mountain plain. The neighboring village is called Dali, but it's not as close as it looks. Your party must cross a treacherous area full of monsters and random battles. If your group is somewhat low on HP and MP, you may want to head to the South Gate first. At this point, Garnet decides to use a different name. From this point forward, she will be referred to as "Dagger."

THE SOUTH GATE

Search the left side of the gate to speak with the guard; you're given several options. You cannot pass through without the Gate Pass, a key item that you won't find until later on. You can rest at the gate for 100 Gil, or the guard will happily chat away about the purpose of the gate and the two kingdoms of Alexandria and Lindblum.

If you go through all of the guard's options at least once, even if you cancel an option, then Part-time Worker Mary appears and offers to sell your crew medicines.
THE OLD MAN AND THE TOWER

MORRID & SPECIAL ITEMS
(keyword:MORRID1)
Before heading into the village, check out the strange mountain close to the edge of the plain. Here you'll find an old fellow named Morrid, who purchased the Mini-Prima Vista from the Treno Auction. He offers Zidane a trade of the figure for some rare coffees, namely Burman, Kirman and Moccha. Keep this in mind as you continue the game. (NOTE: You must bring all three coffees back to Morrid before the end of Disc 3.) You can also find some shipping instructions in his hut. Outside, search near the cart for a Hi-Potion, and check near the fence by the cliff for 135 Gil. There's a device at the top of the mountain that defies explanation.
