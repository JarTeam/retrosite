THE ELUSIVE PRINCESS

Kidnapping Princess Garnet is the goal of the next segment, but the little lady is more than a handful! As Zidane, run straight up the steps to find Garnet. Looking in any other place may result in capture by the palace guards!

When word of Garnet's disappearance reaches the Queen, Captain Steiner of the Knights of Pluto is ordered to find the Princess. As Steiner, the objective is to rally the guards under him and search for Garnet. However, the Knights of Pluto aren't exactly a tip-top outfit, so as Steiner you must be forceful to get things going!

Follow Blutzen and Kohel into the room where Zidane and Blank stole their clothes. Order them to get out, and then search near the door for a Phoenix Down. On the opposite side is a list of all the Knights of Pluto; use this as a reference to determine when you've given them all orders. Also, the moogle Mosh is nearby to save your game if desired.

Run into the main hall and follow the balcony to the right. You'll find a hungry Mullenkedheim in the banquet hall. The soldier doesn't spring into action, but at least the orders have been received. As Steiner is leaving the banquet hall, the knight Dojebon should run smack into the Captain. Don't let 'em get away! Make sure that Dojebon is searching for the Princess.

On the lowest level, you can find Pluto Knight Laudo behind the last bookcase in the left library.

Now run out to the courtyard and find Haagen dipping his feet in the canal. Near the right-hand gate is an unnamed Knight of Pluto. In front of the left-hand gate is Weimar, trying to hustle one of Beatrix's troops! Proceed through the left-hand gate, and start climbing up the virtually endless spiraling stairs of the tower. Halfway up, Steiner should pass an over-exerted Breireicht. Talk to this soldier a second time. If you have given orders to all of the Knights of Pluto by this point, Breireicht gives Steiner an Elixir!

THE QUEEN'S TEMPER


Continue up the stairs to the top of the tower, where Steiner spots Zidane chasing the Princess. Following one of the greatest chase scenes in videogame history, you must pursue Garnet through the Prima Vista. After falling through the trap door into the Engine Room, face the large metal structure and turn the wheel to the left and to the right. Two items, a Phoenix Down and a Phoenix Pinion, will fall to the ground. When the Princess finally gives in, lead her through the hold and back up to the stage. There, Garnet gives a splendid performance in Tantalus's act. Unfortunately, Vivi is chased onstage and reveals her true identity to all!

The battle with Steiner over the Princess is actually split into three confrontations, resulting in an explosive finish. The first confrontation takes place in the hold of the Prima Vista, with the party consisting of Zidane, Cinna, and Blank. Have Zidane and Blank attack while Cinna steals every round.
The second skirmish takes place onstage, but this time Steiner is joined by Haagen and Weimar. Luckily, Garnet and Vivi add their magic-using abilities to Zidane's and Marcus's might. Marcus is still equipped with his SFX gear, so he and Zidane should attack every round. Have Vivi cast Fire on Steiner repeatedly while Garnet casts Cure where necessary. Don't worry about the two guards: they tuck their tails and run when the might of the characters is displayed.
The battle continues after Queen Brahne launches a Bomb monster to destroy the Prima Vista. Continue to use the strategy mentioned earlier as the heroes try desperately to make Steiner turn around. Only when Steiner's HP gets depleted will the big lug finally notice the Bomb monster behind him.
