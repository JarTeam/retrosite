BAKU'S CREW

As the Prima Vista soars over the city of Alexandria preparing to land, our hero Zidane enters the lower cabin. After he strikes a match, you can walk around the cabin and find 47 Gil and a Potion. Also, make note of the information contained in the Prima Vista's Registration plaque, because you'll be tested on it later! When you're finished, light the candle on the table, and the rest of the crew will join Zidane.

Baku surprises his crew by leaping at them in a Dragon mask. When this occurs, you will command a party of four characters. Order each of them to attack, with the exception of Cinna. Cinna's attacks are somewhat weak, so make Cinna steal from Baku instead. You should be able to steal a Potion, a Wrist, and a Mage Masher before the other three brigands manage to chop Baku's mask in half.
