SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY

After the heroes spend the night at the Dali Inn, Zidane wakes up to find that everyone else has wandered off. A few ATEs ensue, revealing the activities of your comrades. Search the room for a Potion and an Antidote before you head out to hook up with them. There's also a Color Fortunes Divination in the room that predicts how your day will unfold, and assigns you a random lucky color for 10 Gil a shot.

The moogle Gumo is in the lobby and you should have a letter for him. Innkeeper Hal is asleep, so head out to find your friends.

The shop across the street sells a lot of useful items, most of which you already have. Still, it's good to double up so that everyone learns Abilities faster, rather than handing around items. (A complete inventory list for the shop is at the end of this section.) Search the plants in the bottom part of the screen to read Shopkeeper Eve's newsletter, which is full of useful info. Eve is a sharp card player, but with some cunning, you can defeat her.

STELLAZZIO COIN COLLECTING
(keyword:STELLA1)
Vivi is near the base of the town's windmill. Approach the Black Mage to engage in some friendly conversation, but then something weird occurs. Regardless, search inside the windmill to find an Aries Stellazzio. There are two more chests up top, but you can't open them unless the windmill shuts down. Also, notice the kid named Yaff, who maybe says too much.

The tavern is across from the windmill, and you can buy medicines there. The Mayor's house is below the windmill, but he chases out anyone who tries to enter his abode. Looks like you'll need to return at another time to get the Mayor's Key needed to unlock the door in the windmill.

As you're searching, ATEs become available that provide some clues as to where to find your friends, and that something isn't quite right in this town. Dagger's at Eve's shop now; talk to her and return to the Inn after this encounter.

After collecting all of the free items and stocking up at the shops, return to the Inn to speak with Dagger again. The two then decide to go looking for the missing Vivi. Leave the Inn and head toward the windmill, where you discover that Vivi is underground. Enter the windmill and open the hatch to go down below.

DARK BUSINESS

Jump off the ladder and take the lift down. Below the lift is a chest containing 156 Gil. Proceed up the passage until Zidane and Dagger overhear two men talking about Vivi. As they lead the little mage off, search inside the pavilion to find a Potion. Across from that treasure chest is a slightly hidden chest with Eye Drops.

Follow Vivi and his captors through a corridor, and then search the barrel to speak with Kumop the moogle. He has a letter for Mogki, and this is a good point to save your game, because things are going to get a little tricky ahead! Just beyond Kumop, kick the wheel to drop a chest containing a Potion, and then climb the crates to reach the chest with the Ether.

ZIDANE & SPECIAL ITEMS
(keyword:MISTMA1)
A larger chamber holds an Iron Helm and a Leather Wrist. Climb the boxes on the top wall to get the first item. In the next room, Dagger and Zidane find a strange machine. Vivi has been stored in a coffin-like box for some reason, and they let him out. There's a door at the top of the area, from which Mist is emanating. The chest on the right has 95 Gil. Opening this door unleashes Mist monsters and random encounters throughout the underground area. However, if you search the room beyond, you can get a Phoenix Down, a Potion and a Phoenix Pinion. Exit and continue through the passage to the right of the machines.

PREPARE VIVI
At some point after rescuing Vivi, make sure you equip him with a Leather Wrist so that he can cast Blizzard. Otherwise, an upcoming tough fight will have you replaying this entire part as punishment for your lack of preparation.

After some mishaps and blunders, the three party members find themselves bound for shipping on a Cargo Airship. Luckily, Steiner is poking around and his nose leads him to the rescue just in time! However, the heroes aren't out of the woods yet; another Black Waltz appears, threatening to destroy the crew and kidnap the Princess!

BLACK WALTZ NO. 2 BOSS FIGHT
(keyword:BWALTZ2)
HP:
1030
AP:
 5
Weak Vs.:
 Wind
Steal Items:
 Steepled Hat, Leather Plate
Spoils:
 Ether, Zaghnol Card (random)
Fire, Blizzard, and Thunder magic should damage Black Waltz No. 2 about equally, but will also provoke him to retaliate with magic in return. Steiner's Swd Mag attacks will not provoke this retaliation while doing much more damage, so it may be better to leave magical attacks to him for this battle. The evil one's goal is to knock out your entire party and hypnotize the Princess.
The Black Waltz will likely cast Thunder, so have Dagger cast Cure each round while the others concentrate on attacking and stealing. Also, have Dagger cast Protect on the other party members (if possible), starting with Vivi.


Following the battle, take Dagger's advice and go rest at the Inn. The Airship will still be there when you're ready to continue. If you move onwards now, you will proceed directly to another Boss fight without collecting any new items or healing your party.
