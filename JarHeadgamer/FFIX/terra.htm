CROSSING OVER

Following the simultaneous defeat of the four Guardians of Terra, it's time to cross dimensions and explore that other world for more answers. Pilot your airship to the Shimmering Island, which is the glowing iceberg just south of the Lost Continent. When you press the Circle button, the party gets drawn off the ship and into the new world.

STRANGERS IN A STRANGE LAND

The ominous Garland greets the party at their landing site on Terra. Select your party and head downward along the path. Random battles with powerful enemies begin almost immediately.

FIGHTING STRATEGY
KEYWORD: ABLTY5
Equip the Devil Killer ability on everyone, and be prepared to cast a lot fire magic.

SPECIAL ITEM
KEYWORD: KEYIT4


A strange blonde girl runs away from Zidane in the next area. The object is to pursue her, but there are valuable items off the path she follows. Don't worry, she'll wait up ahead. For now, head down the steps and follow the path around the pond to the far side, stopping off at treasure chests along the way to collect a Coronet and finally a Dragon Wrist. Then return to the main path and go after the girl. A chest along the way contains an Elixir.

Follow the mysterious girl over an alien bridge, and then climb down the webbing at the end. There is another web directly below the first one that leads down to a platform with a Remedy. Climb back up and follow the ledge around to the gap at the left. Jump across the gap and search for the hidden trunk around the corner, which contains a Mythril Racket. From this side of the gap, you have the option to climb down the webbing.

On the lower level is a Demon's Vest in a trunk, and around the corner, Zidane attempts to confront the blonde-haired girl once more. After a few enigmatic phrases, she leads Zidane onto another bridge and encourages him to go ahead. At the end of the bridge is a stairway leading up to a platform. A nondescript tunnel on the left side of this platform spirals down to a chest containing the Minerva's Plate. Continue up the stairs to the right to reach Bran Bal.
