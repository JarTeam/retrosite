A LITTLE LOST...

The young black mage named Vivi seems a little lost in Alexandria. You'll need a few tips to get your bearings, and you can greatly boost your inventory in no time at all.

In the first alley, there are 2 Potions at different locations. Search the outside of the house to the left for a Potion. You can find Grandma through the bottom-right door, hard at work on a dress for tonight's play. Search around this small abode to find Grandma's measly savings (9 Gil), a Potion, and a Fang Card.

Upon leaving Grandma's hut, travel back the way Vivi came into Memorial Square. The front of the central statue bears an inscription, and behind it is a Potion. Search around the outer perimeter to find a Zombie Card, a Lizard Man Card, and a Sahagin Card. Now that you have a nice little collection going, return to Vivi's path and continue upward through the streets.

In the areas where the nobles are marching off, search in the grass close to the bottom of the screen for 33 Gil and a Goblin Card. Then duck inside the Morning Star Bar and search for a Potion, a Flan Card and 27 Gil. Whew! Is Vivi big enough to carry all that?

Continuing into the next section, talk to Mick about Vivi's Ticket, and then cross the street to Doug's Item Shop. Doug sells medicines, so purchase some Antidotes and Eye Drops to bring Vivi's inventory up to par. You can search the shop to find some 38 Gil, if that helps any.

The ticket booth is in the center of Alexandria's Main Square. This is where Vivi learns that his ticket is a fake. The Ticketmaster feels bad and gives Vivi Goblin, Fang and Skeleton Cards in return. You also learn about a person named "Alleyway Jack," who knows a great deal about cards but doesn't seem to be around town today.

Near the castle gates is Hippaul, who tells Vivi that he had hidden his stash of cards in a secret place. Search directly across the street from Hippaul to find a Phoenix Pinion. Also, if Vivi jumps rope with the girls 20 times or more, he will be rewarded with items.

The building on the far right side of Alexandria Square is a Synthesist. They won't offer to create any new weapons at this time, but you can pick up an Ether near the counter. The weapons smiths are in the connecting room, but they don't seem to be selling to Vivi either. However, you can find a Remedy by searching in front of the arrow sign. On the opposite side of the Square is a hotel, but there's no vacancies and not much else is going on there. To the left of the hotel is an alley.

MISCHIEF AFOOT

While running down the alley, Vivi suddenly trips and falls near Dante the signmaker and his ladder. When the rat-boy Puck offers to get Vivi in to see the show, you should initially refuse his request. If you do, Vivi can find Hippaul's secret stash of cards (Ironite, Goblin, and Fang) by ringing the bell in the Steeple. Once you agree to be his slave, Puck then steals the ladder, and leads Vivi down a sunny street to an old bell-tower.
HIPPAUL'S SECRET
(keyword: HIPCDS1)
When the rat-boy Puck offers to get Vivi in to see the show, you should initially refuse his request. If you do, Vivi can find Hippaul's secret stash of cards (Ironite, Goblin, and Forest Fang) by ringing the bell in the Steeple.


MUGGER...OR CARD GURU?
Don't remain in the alley too long after Puck runs off, you could get mugged and lose some Gil. However, if you speak to this person before he mugs you, you'll find that you've discovered "Alleyway Jack"! A brief tutorial follows, where the card game "Tetra Master" is explained. This game is also explored in the Tetra Master section contained herein. Alleyway Jack also lets you play him, and chances to defeat him and gain a strong card are extremely good.

There's a house near the bell tower where Vivi can find Eye Drops next to the stove. A little girl blocks Vivi from going upstairs, but if you wait outside for a moment, the little girl runs out! Note that when you search upstairs, all you find is a paltry sum of 3 Gil. Next to this house, the window to the right of the door will display the top three jumping rope scores.
PET FINDER
(keyword: MITTEN1)
There's a child named Tom who is looking for his cat, Mittens, at the docks. Agree to look for the pet, and speak to Tom again to learn that the cat is brown. Return to Memorial Square and "speak" to the brown cat behind the central statue. Tom will appear and retrieve the cat. Revisit Tom at the docks to receive a strong Bomb Card.
 

Inside the bell tower, Puck and Vivi bump into the friendly moogles named Kupo and Stiltzkin. The moogles found throughout the game enable you to save your game or use Tents to rest up and restore your HP and MP. Kupo also has a letter that needs to be delivered to Monty. Offer to deliver letters for the Mognet, and purchase items from Stiltzkin whenever he appears. After getting to know the moogles, search the bushes to the left and right for a Tent and a Potion. Afterward, join Puck at the top of the tower.

Puck leads Vivi across the rooftops of Alexandria. Don't rush to catch up, but instead take the time to search the chimneys off the path to find a few stashes of Gil. As Vivi and Puck make it inside the castle, the play begins!


PERFORMANCE OF A LIFETIME

The Tantalus performing troupe puts on quite a show, eh? During the scene where Zidane and the gang fight Baku and the others, everyone is equipped with Special FX (SFX) to liven up the event. These amazing pyrotechnics make it look like spells are being cast, but no damage is inflicted. Eliminate the two guards immediately with one blow each. Baku proves to be a bit tougher, though.

Next up, Blank and Zidane run into the audience and begin a choreographed stage fight. You must follow Blank's lead by pushing the buttons he tells you to push-very quickly. Accuracy and speed are both factors in how well things go.

At the end, Blank and Zidane run into the wings for a moment while the audience approval is tallied. The number of nobles impressed by the stage fight dictates whether or not the Queen is impressed as well. Luckily, you can try this event over and over until you succeed, but you will earn more Gil if the audience is pleased on the first try. If 100 of the nobles are impressed, as well as the Queen, the party stands to make up to 10,000 Gil!
