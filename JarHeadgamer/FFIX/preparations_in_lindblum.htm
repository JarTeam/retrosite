THE SILENCE OF SORROW

After the rescue, Zidane awakes in the guest room at Lindblum. The chests are all full of items, so make sure you pillage them and speak to Mogki. He has a letter for you to deliver to Moodon over at the Business District Inn. The chests contain an Egoist's Armlet and an Elixir.

As you head toward the stairs, Blank enters. If you ask him about Dagger, he'll tell you she's by the telescope on the roof. If you select the other option, he remembers to tell you to head to the Regent's throne room. Either way, you can speak to Dagger first or proceed directly to the throne room. You can also find your friends waiting in the conference room before going to see Cid.

There are some ATEs you can view here. One in particular, "My Bad," can be viewed only if you choose the "Where's Dagger?" option in a conversation with Blank. Also, if you return to the room after choosing "Where's Dagger?" and talk to Blank again, you won't be able to view the ATE "My Bad."

Leave the conference room and return to the mid level of the castle. Return to the guest room, where everyone expresses their concern for the princess. When the meeting recommences in the throne room, everyone discusses plans to return Regent Cid to human form. It's up to Zidane to search Lindblum for three items: Unusual Potion, Strange Potion, and Beautiful Potion.

A FLASK FULL OF HOPE

Cinna has the Unusual Potion, and the ATE that follows the throne room scene shows that he is helping repair the Tantalus hideout. Board the air cab and ride from the castle to the Theater District.

Outside the station, Zidane runs into the family he saved in Burmecia. However, if you ask them, they don't know anything about the potions you're looking for. Before going to Tantalus, visit Michael's studio to the right. Ask the artist about it, and he will tell you to search his studio. Search the corner by the stairs to find the Strange Potion. Also, open the treasure chest in here to get a Lapis Lazuli.
.

Now visit the Tantalus hideout. During the short scene, Cinna gladly gives Zidane the Unusual Potion. But after Zidane leaves the area, you may want to double-back and search inside the hideout. The chests inside now contain 1273 Gil, 4826 Gil, and 970 Gil. If you completed the Mr. Morrid's Coffee side quest, then the Mini-Prima Vista will be on the floor to the left under the loft.

Now there's just one more potion to find, and it's somewhere in the Business District. Ride the air cab there, and don't forget to give the letter to Moodon at the Inn. Also, if Zidane checks the register near the counter, he will jot down his own opinion of the Inn.

STELLAZIO COIN COLLECTING
KEYWORD: STELLA09
The Gysahl Pickle cart is back in business. If you search the ground in the space to the left of the cart, you'll discover the Sagittarius Stellazzio on the ground. Return to the Card Freak's house, where the chests now contain an Elixir and a Remedy.

Continue into the market square, where Alice's shop is under reconstruction. Ask Alice about the potion and she'll hand it over! Now with the Beautiful Potion in hand, you're ready to return to the castle. But don't you think you owe it to yourself to do some shopping first? The Synthesist is still open, and Dragoos now sells medicine for Alice, in addition to the usual weapons and armor. Complete inventories are at the end of this section.

With the three potions in hand, return to Regent Cid's throne room in the castle. Unfortunately, the mixture has a different effect on Cid than what was hoped for. However, the Regent is undaunted and continues the meeting from where it left off.

Cid and the party determine that the group should return to the Black Mage Village on the Outer Continent to determine the connection between the mages and Kuja. Leave the throne room and take the elevator to the base level. Ride the trolley on the right side to the Serpent's Gate. On the landing platform, the chest hidden at the back now contains a Chimera Armlet. Outside, the Blue Narciss is docked at the end of the pier.

Before you board the Blue Narciss, there's a chance to see another ATE called "Give Me My Money!" You can view this ATE only if you didn't pay the 100 Gil to Grandma Pickle for the pickle that Quina ate. Thus, to view this ATE, you must be carrying less than 100 Gil at the time of payment.
.

THE BLUE NARCISS

Once onboard the Blue Narciss, Quina rejoins the party and Cid tells you to select your party members.

SWITCHING PARTY MEMBERS
If you want to switch out the fighting team, press the Triangle button while on the World Map on the ship and speak with Cid to select new party members.

The controls for the ship briefly appear on-screen; they're also listed here for your convenience. You can now visit any beach on the World Map. The only new outside town you can visit is Esto Gaza, which is on the Lost Continent, covered with ice (it lies to the north).

Since the adventure will take the heroes there soon enough, Esto Gaza is covered later. However, if you have a lot of Gil to spend and you'd like to go shopping, there are some worthwhile items to get there. Refer to the "Esto Gaza" section of the Walkthrough if you're interested.

You can also visit Alexandria by landing at the harbor on the eastern side of the Mist Continent. There are a few items scattered amongst the debris, and a few changes in the locations of the shops and salesmen. Skip forward to the "Alexandria Fallen" section to learn more about this.
