STEALING ITEMS
Zidane can steal items from enemies, and you can improve and refine this skill by learning certain abilities. He can eventually steal better items (Master Thief) and inflict damage while stealing (Mug). Until you learn such abilities, use the 'Detect' skill to spot any items the enemies may possess.
