*Additional Note

*Battle starts with 3 Tonberries in front of a chosen character(chosen randomly).
*It'll advance 1 step forward when it's its turn.
*If a character is on its way, it'll cast "Everyone's Grudge."
*Once it reaches to the target, it'll use Knife.
*It'll use Knife after the character dies(misses).
*During the next round, it'll self-destruct and disappear.
*If the character that's in front of Tonberry attacks, it'll counter by advancing 2 steps forward.
*If the character on the right attacks, it'll counter by moving to the right.
*If the character on the left attacks, it'll counter by moving to the left.
*If a character is on its way, it'll bump and goes back(?)
