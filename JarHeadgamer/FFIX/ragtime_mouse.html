SIDEQUESTS > SECRETS >The Ragtime Mouse
 


While wandering the forests of the world, you may encounter a strange creature that asks your party true or false questions. To answer a question, attack the large "circle" for "True" or "X" for "False."

For each correct answer, you receive a percentage grade based on the number of questions you've answered right. For each correct answer, you receive 1,000 Gil.

To locate the Ragtime Mouse, search in most any forest area. Note that the Ragtime Mouse is different from the friendly creatures, and you don't have to appease this creature to receive the Friendly Yan's blessing.
