ONE IS ALL, ALL IS ONE

Upon boarding the Hilda Garde 3, Zidane and the party review the clues uncovered in the Taharka's chamber. The party must conquer all four locations at the exact same time. This means splitting the party off into pairs and depositing them at the four shrines, located in four different locations around the world. The only challenge to this is finding the four shrines and defeating one of the four shrine guardians.

The shrine closest to Ipsen's Castle is the Water Shrine, located in the waters in the center of the "hook" on the Forgotten Continent. Let the airship hover over the area and press the Circle button to drop off Dagger and Eiko.

The Fire Shrine is located north of Esto Gaza, on the Lost Continent. Fly into the volcano and press the Circle button to release Amarant and Freya.

As Zidane indicates, the next location is the Wind Shrine, located on the southeastern part of the Forgotten Continent. Press the Circle button to assign Vivi and Steiner to attack this shrine.

The Earth Shrine is located in the grassy area on the eastern part of the Outer Continent. Hover above the quaking area and press the Circle button to enter the shrine.
The game follows Zidane's and Quina's adventure, with brief cut-scenes to highlight the plights of the others.

FOCUS ON THE EARTH SHRINE

After the duo converse at the entrance, take one last chance to ensure that both are equipped with their strongest weapons. Ipsen's Castle has a way of leaving you with a weak armament, and you don't want that here.

Also, take this opportunity to equip the following abilities on both characters, if possible: Auto-Float, Auto-Regen or Auto-Potion, High Tide, and Counter. Given the number of stones needed to equip all those abilities, it's a pretty tall order. They are listed in order of priority, in case you can't equip them all. Also, equip Quina with any items that absorb Earth damage.

When you're ready, proceed down the steps. As the mismatched pair makes their way down the corridor, a trap springs causing the walls to close from different directions. Press the X button when a "!" appears over Zidane's head to outmaneuver the sliding stones. This may take a few tries, but don't worry, there is no penalty for any errors made.

In the main chamber, follow Quina and examine the altar. At the same instant in all four shrines, the characters come face-to-face with the Guardians of Terra, the protectors of the four shrines. The only one you'll actually fight is the Earth Guardian.

 EARTH GUARDIAN

EARTH GUARDIAN BOSS FIGHT TIPS
KEYWORD: ERTHG1
HP:
20,756

 11
Weak Vs.:
 Wind, Holy
Steal Items:
 Rubber Suit, Avenger
Spoils:
 Phoenix Pinion
In the first two rounds, have Quina cast Mighty Guard on him/herself and Zidane, and then cast Bad Breath on the Guardian each round until it succumbs to Mini status. This weakens the monster seriously in terms of both attack and defense. If you don't have those abilities, then have Quina use 1,000 Needles or Aqua Breath.
Have the character who lives through the Earth Guardian's spell attacks use a Phoenix Down on the other, and hold out until Zidane Trances. At that point, use Grand Lethal once or twice to bring down this beast. Quina can eat this Boss to finish the battle, learning Earth Shake in the process. However, you should hold off until Zidane successfully steals its rare possessions.
