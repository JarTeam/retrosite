THE RAVAGES OF WAR

Lindblum is in shambles. Queen Brahne's attack force has left most of the town in tatters, and the Industrial District is completely wiped out. Move Zidane and Dagger through the Business District. The Alexandrian Soldiers will not attack, and you may speak to them if you want.

The destruction has caused a few items to become scattered in amongst the debris. In the Card Freak's house, there's an Ether and a Phoenix Pinion. Search the wreckage just to the left of that house to find the Lindblum Card.

Proceeding into the mall area, Minister Artania finds Dagger and Zidane and escorts them to Regent Cid. A plan is formed to find and defeat Kuja on the Outer Continent. You must travel there via a series of underground caverns known as "Fossil Roo." Cid gives the party 3,000 Gil to help in the cause.

REMAINS OF THE CITY

Thereafter, a man awaits for you to prepare yourself in the Business District's mall. When you're ready, return to this man and tell him you're all set to go.

SPECIAL ITEMS
(keyword:ITMS5)

You'll want to visit Dragoos, who has tripled his prices for the Alexandrian forces but will give the good guys a discount. Alice's shop is completely destroyed, but she will still sell you potions and medicines. The Synthesist can now forge a few more items than before, but of particular interest is Zidane's Exploda weapon, which will help immensely in the near future. If you don't yet have a Cachusha, then forge this item now so that Dagger can cast Life and resurrect fallen comrades. Naturally, complete inventory lists appear at the end of this section.

The Lindblum Inn is still standing, and the price is still 100 Gil to rest. Fortunately, you can find some extra Gil directly to the left as you enter. Upstairs, Moodon the moogle has an important letter for Zidane from Ruby about recruiting Lowell for her theater in Alexandria. The air cab system will still take you to the Theatre District for free, but signs posted indicate that a fare will be implemented by the queen shortly.

In the Theatre District, check in Michael's art studio for some Ore. The actor Lowell is located just below the Tantalus theater. If Zidane has read Ruby's letter, then he will convince Lowell to take off for Alexandria.

Inside the hideout, Baku and Tantalus have collected a bunch of loot since you last visited, so open all the chests for 340, 262, and 993 Gil. After this, there's not much left for you in old Lindblum. Time to set off for the Qu's Marsh north of the conquered city, where a rumored excavation site may take you to the Outer Continent.

At the Lindblum Grand Castle's base level, Regent Cid will give your group the World Map to help. The Merchant remains stationed at the Dragon's Gate, selling a combination of items and potions. Moonte the moogle will happily save your game before you head off once more.
