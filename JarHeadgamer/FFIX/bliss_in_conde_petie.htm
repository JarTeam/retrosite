PRENUPTIAL ARRANGEMENTS o

Return to Conde Petie on the double. Move through town and find Shamis Gatekeeper, the dwarf blocking the exit from the ceremonial area. He's a bit more forthcoming with information this time, and directs Zidane to find the village priest.

You can find Father David Heavenguard wandering the corridor between the entrance and the Inn. Zidane learns from him that only those who are married in the village can go west from here. Dagger walks in and instantly agrees to undergo the ceremony with Zidane.

Following the crazy nuptials and depending on if you chose the second option in this section, Vivi and Quina decide to get married, too. You will then be given the option to watch an ATE of Vivi and Quina's marriage. Is Quina so happy to be married, or because s/he might get to eat Vivi? If you chose the first option, they will just sneak past the guards.
