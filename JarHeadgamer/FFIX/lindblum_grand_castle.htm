THE CASTLE CITY

After disembarking the Cargo Airship, follow Minister Artania through the castle's lobby to the lift. Pay close attention to Artania, so you can grasp something about the layout of this awesome city. You're then escorted into the chambers of Regent Cid, who is related to the Princess.

While lunch is served, Zidane heads into the Industrial District to sample some of the less high-brow fare, and maybe meet a few ladies. However, he runs into Freya instead, an old comrade-in-arms hailing from the nearby kingdom of Burmecia.

Zidane wakes up the next day at the Business District Inn. After another tutorial about Synthesis, head into the next room to speak to the moogle Moodon, who has a letter from Ruby. In the lobby of the inn, there's a guestbook and a bust of a Bobo Bird. Search in the corner next to the bust (closest to the exit) for 163 Gil.

Upon exiting the Inn, a series of optional and mandatory ATEs will indicate what the others are doing. Although Zidane's goal is to head to the Theater District to look for Tantalus, there are some great benefits in fully searching the rest of the city beforehand.

Directly across from the Inn is the air cab terminal, where cars wait to take you to Lindblum Castle, the Industrial District or the Theater District. At the bottom of the screen is the Hunter's Gate, where you can walk out into the world. Be advised that it's not a good idea to leave Lindblum alone. The monsters outside the city are too ferocious for one warrior to take on alone. If you head the other way down the street, past the Inn and the air cab terminal, you'll reach the market square.

Positioned around the market are several vendors, including an action figure stand. The door next to Ivan's Fish Shack leads upstairs into Card Freak Gon's house. He's very sharp, so only take him on if you've been playing the card game rather regularly up to this point. You can find a Hi-Potion and an Echo Screen in the chests near the balcony. You can also check out an issue of Card Collector on the bookshelves. Different volumes are on display at different points in the game, so you might return here later in the game to witness some different tips.

The church is down the street beyond Gon's house. Search below the tree branches just under the church to find a Tent. Inside, climb up the scaffold and search the near corner for a Leather Plate. Now return to the market square and take the right-hand street to keep exploring.

The second street from the market square leads to a mall with a large sundial fountain. On the right side is the Dragoos Armory, where you can buy a variety of new and familiar weapons, armor, and accessories. (For a complete inventory list, see the end of this section.) The store at the top of the mall is Alice's Item Shop, where Vivi acquires a Kupo Nut. Alice also sells a greater assortment of medicines, all listed at this section's end as well.

TORRES AND THE SYNTH SHOP
(keyword:TORRES1)
Continue past Alice's shop to the left to find Torres's Synthesis Shop. Go behind the counter and search Torres's workbench to find some Silver Gloves, and then return to the customer side of the counter to see what's available. You may be able to forge the Butterfly Sword easily, but it's extremely important that you forge The Ogre for Zidane as well. If you don't have enough Mage Mashers, you can purchase them fairly cheap at Dragoos's Shop. All of the items on the list are worth synthesizing, so go crazy!

THE OGRE
Don't leave Lindblum at this point in the game without forging The Ogre. This double-bladed weapon has the Soul Blade ability, which will be useful in an upcoming Boss fight.

THE INDUSTRIAL DISTRICT

This smaller area of Lindblum is where most of the city's airship engineers dwell. Some businesses once existed here, but they all pulled out and moved into the more lucrative Business District, such as Dragoos' Shop.

Directly beside the entrance to the terminal is a chest containing a Leather Wrist. Behind the statue of Cid VIII, you might find a man named Marolo, who loves to debate. Take his side in the debate for now, because you'll meet this person again in another town later and it's good to take his side. For now, agree that putting the engine in the back results in more power to make him happy.

Head up the stairs into the next section, where you'll come across Bobo's familiar dive. Search next to the pub to receive a Bronze Vest. There's a house in the back of this neighborhood, wherein Ludruff the engineer is working on a prototype steam engine. Search in the upstairs loft to find a Mimic Card and a Steepled Hat. There's not much else to do in the Industrial District; it's really just a cozy neighborhood. Head back to the air cab and ride to the Theater District now.

THE THEATER DISTRICT

Outside the air cab terminal, look in the bushes in the foreground (where Zidane is shown standing) to find 127 Gil. There's a boy nearby named Tim who knows that most of the people in the Theater District play cards. So, brush the dust off your deck and challenge a few folks, because there are some rare cards to win.

Continue down the steps to a building marked with a clock. This is the Tantalus troupe's playhouse, but no one's home. Zidane will park it and wait for a bit; in the meantime, an ATE about Dagger occurs. Afterward, Zidane has a brief discussion with the kids Lucella and Bunce, who suggest that he search for the princess. Now that we know where she is, that's not too much of a problem. Then proceed to ransack the Tantalus playhouse for 68 Gil, 97 Gil, 282 Gil, and a Mini-Burmecia up in the loft.

LOWELL AND THE MOOGLE SUIT
(keyword:LOWELL1)
Leave the Tantalus playhouse and head down the steps. Down here you'll find a ravenous crowd of fans waiting for their heartthrob, Lowell. The actor makes a brief appearance, but then leaves. Meanwhile, a man in a moogle suit emerges and makes his way up the stairs. Follow him back to the area outside the air cab terminal. There's an artist living in a cellar across from the terminal. Visit there now, and you'll find Lowell, who's hiding from his fans. You can receive his Autograph, and then search the area directly across from his position for the Moogle Suit. Also, pick up the Ore from the nearby chest.

DAGGER IN DESPAIR

Take an air cab to Lindblum Castle. In the lobby, take the red-carpeted stairs up to the right, and then go through the doorway on the balcony to reach the guest room where Dagger was seen previously. This is where Zidane reunites with "Rusty," who's throwing another fit about the princess. Once Steiner leaves, search the room for a Glass Armlet and an Ether on the upper balcony. Also, you'll encounter Mogki the moogle, to whom you can give a letter. She will also ask you to deliver a letter to Atla; keep that Mognet going!

As you return to the lobby, Zidane hears Dagger's sweet and melancholy voice. However, the guard at the lift isn't letting a miscreant like Zidane get past. Go back up the steps toward the guest room, but then head down the next set of steps and wake up the sleeping soldier. Zidane takes the guard out of sight and pounds him, taking the uniform as a disguise. Now return to the lobby, and you'll have no problem getting past the guard. Operate the control on the left side of the lift to reach the Upper Level.

Zidane switches back to street clothes on the ride up, so that Dagger will immediately recognize him. However, Zidane must avoid the guards as well; if he gets too close to one, you can watch an additional mini-scene. Take the stairs to the left up through an engine room to the top of the tower. The guard up here won't mind if Zidane talks to Dagger.

There's a telescope nearby, and while looking through it you can spot six locations before the scene moves on (a Marshland, the Aerbs Mountains, South Gate, the mountain between Lindblum and Alexandria, the Chocobo Forest, and the Ceebell River). Viewing them marks some of them on the Continental Map for later use.

THE FESTIVAL OF THE HUNT

A montage of scenes ensues, during which the monsters for the Festival of the Hunt are accidentally released early. Inside the guest room, the rules of the festival are explained. You receive points for each monster killed within the time limit. As you fight, your score is displayed in the bottom-right corner of the screen. The top score and whom it belongs to is displayed on top.

The winner of the Festival of the Hunt receives the Master Hunter award. Each character also specifies an additional prize that they want: if Vivi wins, you receive a Theater Ship Card; if Freya wins, you get a Coral Ring; and if Zidane wins, you'll get 5,000 Gil.

FESTIVAL OF THE HUNT
(keyword:FESTIV1)
Below are two proven ways for Zidane to win the contest. Just kill the monsters in the order listed, and you should win either way you choose. The difference is in whether or not you choose to fight the Zaghnol, a monster that is almost Boss-like in strength. Because it takes longer to defeat the Zaghnol, you may not want to risk it.

To begin the contest, equip Zidane with the strongest weapon and armor available. Also, equip the Beast Killer ability to help. If you synthesized the Yellow Scarf at Torres's Synthesis Shop, the Bird Killer ability will be helpful as well. Then take the air cab to the Theater District.

THE SYNTH SHOP
Make sure you stop by the Synth Shop before leaving, because you can pick up a new weapon for Zidane - The Ogre!

Disembark at the Theater District, and defeat the Mu chasing the person in the first area. Then wait for the Trick Sparrow in this same area to swoop down on Zidane. There's another Trick Sparrow outside the Tantalus theater, but it takes too long and isn't worth a lot of points. Run down the steps to the lower square and stand by the barrels, because a Fang will emerge and leap over the barricade to attack.

Return to the air cab and travel to the Industrial District. Tackle the Fang chasing the cat around the statue, and then take out the Mu on the stairs. Check in the doorway of Bobo's Pub to attack another Mu, and then defeat the Mu near the top of the area.

Take the air cab to the Business District to finish things. There are two ways you can win here. You can kill the Trick Sparrow outside the Inn, and then the Mu that leaps off the stairs on the right. Attack the Fang directly across from the pickle stand, and then decide whether or not you want to challenge the Zaghnol. If you do, skip to the next paragraph. If not, then head left past Card Freak Gon's house and battle yet another Fang. Battle the Trick Sparrow near the church, and then return to the square, where you'll find Vivi being chased by a Fang. Save your buddy, and by then time should run out.

If you're going after the glory of defeating the Zaghnol as well, then skip the church section and move toward the sundial square instead. There, Freya will leap to Zidane's aid in an attempt to save the townsfolk from the gigantic beast.

During each round, command Freya to jump and have Zidane perform a regular attack. If Freya is kept in the air, she's less likely to take damage than Zidane, so if something happens to Zidane, have her serve as a medic. This battle can take as long as two to three minutes to win, so only attempt it if you're desperate or have plenty of time.

ZIDANE LOSES
If by chance you follow this strategy and lose, then Freya will most likely win and your party will get the Coral Ring instead of the Gil. However, if you want the Theater Ship card, then head from the Theater District to the Business District instead, and save Vivi from the Fang. Then return to the Industrial District and do a really bad job of fighting. This way, Vivi should win and you'll get the card.

GRAVE MESSENGER


Following the Festival, the Master Hunter trophy is awarded to one of the three heroes who participated. In addition, they receive the prize of their choice. The celebration is aborted, however, when a wounded soldier from Burmecia drops dead after delivering a shocking message. Afterward, Dagger and Steiner flee from Lindblum, leaving Zidane and Vivi to go with Freya to Burmecia. Make sure your party is completely stocked up before leaving.

When your crew is ready to resume the adventure, take the Lindblum Castle lift to the lower level. The left trolley goes only to the Dragon's Gate, from which you need to depart, but the right trolley takes you to the Serpent's Gate first, where you can find a Wyerd Card. Then you can go straight from there to the Dragon's Gate.

At this exit, you'll meet Moonte the moogle, who just received a letter from Stiltzkin. It's recommended that you save your game before leaving. There's also a Merchant nearby, who sells items for decent prices.
