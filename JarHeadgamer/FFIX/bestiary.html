Key
Status Effects Section
X
 An "X" next to a status effect indicates that the monster is immune to it. For example, there is an "X" next to the status effect "Silence" for Black Waltz No. 1. This mean that Black Waltz No. 1 cannot be Silenced.
O
 The "O" on the status effect "Safety" means that the monster cannot be defeated by instant-kill abilities such as "Death" or "Doom."
Attacks/Abilities Section
O
 All abilities with an "O" next to them can be reflected back to the caster if the support ability "Return Magic" or the "Reflect" magic is active. For example, if Vivi has Return Magic equipped and Abadon casts Thundaga on Vivi, Vivi will cast Thundaga back at Abadon. (Note: There is a "O" next to Thundaga for Abadon.) Reflect has the same effect, except you don't take any damage from the spell.
