Q What is PlayOnline?
A PlayOnline is Square's totally new network service, designed to provide a wide range of media and information, including online games, music, comics, and sports news and statistics. PlayOnline's dedicated browser gives you access to all of these services, as well as Messenger (PlayOnline's e-mail service), online chats, and internet web browsing.

Q When will PlayOnline be available?
A We anticipate beginning PlayOnline's services in 2002 for the North American territory. Keep checking back with PlayOnline.com for the latest information.

Q What's the difference between PlayOnline and PlayOnline.com?
A PlayOnline.com will provide you with the latest information on PlayOnline, the FINAL FANTASY game series, and so forth. Please be aware that PlayOnline.com is separate from the services provided by PlayOnline itself.

Q Will you only be able to play FINAL FANTASY XI online?
A FINAL FANTASY XI is a full-fledged network RPG, and as such, you will need to be connected to PlayOnline in order to play. We hope you'll enjoy the totally new style of gameplay and communication unique to this online RPG.

Q Will I enjoy FINAL FANTASY XI online as a multiplayer game?

Once FINAL FANTASY XI is launched on PlayOnline, you will be able to find many fellow players from all over the world. We at Square are doing our best to design and balance the gameplay so that it will be fun and comfortable for everyone. We'll see you Online!
