LOST HOME OF THE SUMMONERS

STELLAZZIO COIN COLLECTING
(keyword:STELLA6)

Follow Eiko into the ancient ruins of the once-powerful village. After the little lady puts Zidane through a detailed screening, open the trunk behind the bench Dagger is seated upon to receive a Tent. Then check on the broken side behind the fountain to find the Libra Stellazzio.

Take the path to the left to talk to Vivi, or head to the right to see what Quina's up to. Don't worry about the two unreachable treasure chests outside the Eidolon Wall, those things will come to you in their own time.

If you attempt to enter Eiko's house, the moogle Morrison will prevent entry. He asks you to follow him back to the Eidolon Wall. Upon hearing the purpose of the wall, Zidane decides to go find Dagger. A previous ATE should have shown you that she is near the village's entrance. When she joins Zidane, head back to speak with Morrison again. During a short scene, the moogle lets them view the mysterious Eidolon Wall.

EIKO'S KITCHEN ACTIVE TIME EVENTS
(keyword:EKCOOK1)
When Eiko is trying to guess how many people to cook for, you must take into account all the moogles and people she lists, plus one more. After all, Eiko doesn't know that Quina made it to Madain Sari! The total number of guests to cook for is 11. And don't even think about seasoning your food with an oglop! Also, when Quina shows up, allow the gourmand to assist in the cooking so that the meal goes well.

LONELY HEARTS

Zidane and Dagger should return to Eiko's house after visiting the Eidolon Wall. Everyone begins eating, and the group discusses Madain Sari, Eiko's abilities, and summoning. Following dinner, Eiko asks Zidane to bring the pot into the kitchen. First, get the Ore and the Phoenix Pinion out of the chests, then grab the pot and follow Eiko out back.

On the back patio, Zidane and Eiko briefly discuss the Iifa Tree. Search near the picnic table on the far left to find the Kirman Coffee. Quina and a moogle are fishing off the lower part, so go back inside. As you're leaving the hut, Momatose enters and offers to let you rest. Why not?

During the night, Vivi and Zidane discuss the importance of having a past, and share a male bonding ritual. In the morning, Eiko decides to join Zidane's group and go to the Iifa Tree. The group leaves Madain Sari.

Before returning to the Mountain Path, roam the area around Eiko's home, defeating enemies, leveling up and learning abilities. The Iifa Tree provides quite a challenge, so it's good to work out and level up. If you need items, return to the lost village and Morrison will greet you at the gate with a full inventory. If the party becomes worn down from excessive battles, you can rest in Madain Sari for free by speaking with Momatose in Eiko's dining room.
