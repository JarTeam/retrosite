THE UNFORGIVING DESERT

Upon disembarking from the Hilda Garde 1, you should stock up on medicine by purchasing it from the jester twins on the ship. You should equip each character with the Clear Headed ability, as well as Loudmouth and Bright Eyes. Now, just summon a moogle and save your game.

The enemies on the Forgotten Continent are much tougher than any you've encountered up to this point. If you stay on your guard, you shouldn't have any trouble. However, the Cactuars sometimes come in quartets when you encounter them in this foreign land, making them a sincerely dangerous enemy to face! Remember to attack them only when they make an "appearance," and don't attack when they "hide." That way, you can avoid any nasty retaliations, such as their 1,000 Needles attack.

QUINA SO HUNGRY!

If you made the choice to bring Quina along, work hard to satisfy that appetite. You can learn Limit Glove by eating Catoblepases or Jabberwocks, and the intimidating Adamantoise will yield the Earth Shake ability. If you haven't done so yet, learn the 1,000 Needles ability by eating a Cactuar.

SEAWAYS CANYON

The day becomes dusky and night threatens to fall when you reach the Seaways Canyon area. This is where you are more likely to encounter Cactuars. Don't be afraid to defeat them, because you may receive a Cactuar Card by successfully defeating three or four of these at once. If you know anything about Tetra Master, these are some of the best cards to get!

To reach Oeilvert, head into the canyons and veer off to the west. This canyon leads your party out to the western seashore. There, another canyon forms that spirals inward to the mysterious fortress, which is surrounded by high canyon walls.
