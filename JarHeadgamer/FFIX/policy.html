Cookies

Cookies are small text files that Web sites send to your computer. A cookie itself does not contain any Personal Information about you. Rather, it is an identifier used by the Web site that placed it on your hard drive to identify the computer and to correlate it with information that you may have provided on the Web site, including Personal Information.

We use cookies for limited purposes. For example, we place a cookie on your computer when you register so that the next time you visit this Site, we can tell you are a user who has registered. This allows us to avoid presenting you additional registration forms. In addition, a cookie may be placed on your computer if you click on one of Square Enix USA's advertising banners on another Web site. Cookies used with advertising banners permits us to count the number of users who clicked on that ad and the site from which they clicked, so that we can measure the effectiveness of our advertisements.

If you have provided Personal Information to us in the registration process, it may be linked to information we collect with cookies, such as whether you are registered, or whether you linked to our Site from an advertising banner.

You may adjust your Web browsers to either alert you of the use of cookies or refuse to accept cookies entirely. For Internet Explorer or AOL users, you can click on the "Start" button and then select Control Panel, Internet Options and the Security tab. If you click on the "Custom" button, you will be able to see the cookie options. For Netscape users, in the browser, select File, Preferences and then Advanced to view the cookie options. Be aware that if you elect not to accept cookies, some parts of the Site may not function properly or permit access.
