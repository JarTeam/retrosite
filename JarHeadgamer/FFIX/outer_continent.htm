THE WASTE LANDS

The Outer Continent is a series of vast desert islands with a few mountains and a couple of scattered forests with small patches of green land scattered about. The first and most notable landmark is a structure suspended on what looks like two gigantic tree roots. Follow the canyon under this structure, and stick to the cliffs as they curve to the east.

To reach the strange building, you must follow the cliffs as they gradually slope downward to meet the lower land level near the eastern shore. Then you can move west up the long slope toward the suspended structure.

The journey through Fossil Roo probably left your party in bad shape, and the trek across the desert is no picnic either. Don't hesitate to use a Tent when needed and save your game. There are strong enemies in the area that might take advantage if your characters are weak.

QUINA & ABILITIES
(keyword:QUIN1)
Have you remembered to feed Quina? Along the desert trip, you'll find a Qu's Marsh in the north where you can stop off and let Quina catch some frogs for prizes. Also, try hard to eat the monsters you encounter. Eating a Griffin teaches Quina the White Wind ability, which restores the entire party's HP. Eat the fierce Cactuar enemy, and not only will your party gain a lot of EXP, AP and Gil, but Quina also learns the 1,000 Needles ability.
