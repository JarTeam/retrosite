THE PARADOX PALACE

The bewildering Ipsen's Castle looks like two castles smashed together, causing one to look upside down while the other part looks right side up. This indicates something about the nature of the place.

NEW WEAPONS
KEYWORD: WEAP3

This is where you can find the weapons: Zidane's Dagger - chest inside foyer; Amarant's Cat's Claws - chest in courtyard; Vivi's Mage Staff - chandelier at the top of the courtyard pole; Quina's Fork - chandelier at the top of the courtyard pole; Freya's Javelin - chest to the left of the gallery below the courtyard; Steiner's Broadsword - chest on circular platform; Dagger's Rod - balcony beneath combination door room.

Outside the entrance, Amarant rebels and decides to go at it on his own. You must now choose three other characters to accompany you inside. The best mixture of magic and physical force seems to be achieved by a party consisting of Zidane, Dagger, Steiner, and Vivi. They seem to be capable of the best teamwork, as well. However, if Quina has learned the LV4 Holy spell, then take him/her along. If Eiko has Madeen, that will also come in handy.

FIGHTING STRATEGIES
KEYWORD: AGGAR4
HERITAGE TEAM
There is a particularly nasty duo you encounter repeatedly here, one in which an Agares uses magic to bring a Gargoyle to life. This is quickly averted by killing the Agares before this can occur. Or, you can use a Soft on the Gargoyle to destroy it instantly.

LIKE WALKING BACKWARDS


STELLAZZIO COIN COLLECTING
KEYWORD: STELLA11

 

Take a deep breath and proceed up the long steps into Ipsen's Castle. Inside the foyer, open the chests hidden behind the pillars on either side for a Dagger and the Aquarius Stellazzio. Head up the steps in the back to venture into a courtyard of sorts.

The moogle Kumool stands in the bottom-left corner of the area. You might have a letter for Kumool from Mogki. Kumool also runs a very convenient Mogshop. Kumool sells Vaccines, so stock up while you have the opportunity!

There's a doorway across the courtyard from Kumool that overlooks an expansive area. As you can see, everything below this level is a mirror image of all that is above. In the courtyard to the left of this doorway, pick up the Cat's Claws from the chest. Slide down the pole on the far-right side of the courtyard.

Move along the arches to the ladder, and climb up to the balcony. Follow the balcony out to a small overlook, and go through the next doorway and hop onto the ladder. Climb up the ladder and press the X button when the icon appears. Select to jump off to the left, run around the gallery, and get the Javelin from the chest. Return to the ladder, but this time jump off to the right. Follow this circular platform to a chest containing a Broadsword.

When you get back to the ladder this time, climb to the bottom and hop off. Cross the lower balcony and climb the steps on the right. Hop on the next ladder and climb up. Jump off this ladder and circle the other side of the balcony to get a Rod. Then climb the ladder to the top, and enter the archway on the top balcony.

SECRET COMBINATION
KEYWORD: OPND5

Move to the bottom of the red carpeted room and examine the fresco on the right. Sure enough, it's a secret door, but it's tough to open. Each time you try something, a new option becomes available. Select the options in the following sequence to open the door:

  • Examine
  • Push
  • Pound
  • Think
  • Push
  • Try something drastic
  • Rest
Leaning on the fresco causes it to open right up, but it's a matter of making the correct options appear. On the right side of the balcony is a Barette. Now go back inside and take the elevator near the back staircase up to the top floor.

A cocky Amarant has beaten Zidane here, and he considers this victory enough to be able to abandon the party. After the rascal stalks away, search the wall in the back and take the Fire Mirror, Water Mirror, Wind Mirror, and the Earth Mirror. Each Key Item is inscribed on the back with a clue as to the location of a shrine.

For a guardian, Taharka seems to fall quite easily under your strongest attacks and magic. Each round, have Vivi cast Flare, while Dagger summons Bahamut or heals the others. Steiner can inflict some more damage by performing Flare Sword.
Taharka casts third-level attack spells, such as Blizzaga and Thundaga, and slashes away at a character in a brutal attack. You can handle this by equipping characters with Auto-Potion or Auto-Regen in advance. Auto-Reflect will come in handy, too.
If given the chance, Taharka will constrict itself and reduce its size. This is a protective measure, and all physical blows fail to cause any sizable damage. Zidane must resort to using skills such as Thievery, Quina can do damage with Frog Drop or 1,000 Needles, and Freya can perform attacks such as Lancer or Six Dragons to do more damage.
The only spoils Taharka yields are 8,092 Gil, which can be more if Quina is in the party and equipped with the Millionaire ability. There is also a chance that you may obtain the Ramuh Card from this battle.

MORE DISCOVERIES WHILE EXITING

Go back out the same way you came in, and climb the pole back up to the courtyard. There is a trap door that is barely visible just below the chest at the top of the courtyard.

Drop through this trap, and Zidane will land on the upside-down chandelier in the dark room below. Collect the Maiden Prayer from the chest, and then hop down and climb the pole back up to the courtyard.

Another trap door is under the balcony on the top-left side. This one drops you onto the upper level of the lower room. You might want to avoid this trap door as there is no reward for triggering it.

Return to the entrance and take note of the staircase that appeared on the left side going up to the top balcony as well. A new entrance is in-between the staircase and the first arch you entered on the right. This leads to the courtyard in the balcony area, and during your trip, look for some chests on the chandelier high above. Continue across the balcony to the upper archway, and descend the steps inside into a whole new area.

There's a chest on the far-left side of this area that contains an Air Racket. Ride the elevator in the center of the room down to find yourself in a room with Odin's electrified sword chained to the back wall. A chest in this area contains a Golem's Flute. There are also two flower vases and three shelves on which to arrange them. Rearrange the vases, moving them from shelf to shelf in a counter clockwise direction. The electrical energies will fill the vase, and then you'll receive the Ancient Aroma. This item teaches Dagger the Odin's Sword ability, which causes the Odin summon to cause damage if it doesn't kill instantly.

You can now return to the courtyard and climb the pole up to the chandelier. The two chests up here contain a Fork and a Mage Staff. But since you're about to leave, these things hardly matter.

THE LOST ALLY

Exit Ipsen's Castle and rejoin your friends at the bottom. Zidane receives the alarming news that Amarant has not yet emerged. As Zidane, return to the courtyard and slide down the pole. Amarant can be heard groaning at the very bottom of the room and rejoins the party. Now, Zidane and Amarant must fight their way back out of the castle. Make sure you equip Amarant with the Cat's Claws beforehand.

Outside the castle, a plan is formed to utilize the four mirrors found inside. The player has the option of exploring more of the World Map, fulfilling a few side quests, or going straight on to the Four Shrines challenge. Just make sure you take the first opportunity to re-equip your characters with the best weapons available!
