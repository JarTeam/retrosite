ANCIENT TREE OF LIFE

Arriving at the massive tree, which may be Kuja's hangout, the group finds the entrance barred by an Eidolon's force field. You can have Zidane poke or body-slam (just once!) the barrier as much as you like to get your kicks. Eventually, you'll want to ask Eiko to intervene.


EIKO & SUMMONING
(keyword:EKSUMM1)
The little summoner breaks the seal and receives a Ruby, which enables her to call upon Carbuncle and cast different spells depending on which Add-on Eiko equips. With the Ruby equipped, Carbuncle casts Ruby Light (casts Reflect on your entire party). Equip a Diamond and Carbuncle casts Diamond Light, which casts Vanish on your entire party. With an Emerald equipped, Carbuncle casts Emerald Light, which casts Haste on your entire party. Equip a Pearl and Carbuncle casts Pearl Light, which casts Shell on your entire party. This is a very versatile and useful summon indeed, so equip it on Eiko immediately. For additional help throughout the Iifa Tree, she may want to know Fenrir and Phoenix.

FIGHTING STRATEGIES
(keyword:UNDED2)

As you travel along the winding tree root, the party will encounter various plant-based and undead enemies weak against Fire. After slowly descending a steep slope, Zidane stumbles across Mocchi the moogle, whom you might have a letter for. From this point forward, you'll encounter mostly undead enemies. Your greatest attack against these types is to cast Cura or Cure, which might even kill them instantly. Or, you can cast Life on them, which kills them instantly.

The group eventually reaches a circular platform with strange markings. Choose for Zidane to stand on it, and he will discover that it is a magical elevator down into the trunk.

ROOTS OF EVIL

As Zidane and the heroes fight their way down the winding roots inside the trunk, you'll eventually come to a pair of offshoots on the right. Go to the bottom one first, and trigger a switch that drops a treasure chest from above. Open the chest to claim a Phoenix Down. There is another offshoot just below there, where a chest holds a Hi-Potion.

Continuing downward, be on the lookout for an Ether in a cave on the right. Just below that location, the path splits so take the left branch before continuing downward. Two chests along this dead-end path contain a Lamia's Flute for Eiko and a Remedy.

The next room displays a kind of express elevator to the bottom of the Iifa Tree. The crew hops on, and the long descent begins. During the ride, you can expect several encounters, but you also have at least one chance to revive characters, equip new items and abilities, or use Potions outside of battle.

ZIDANE & NEW ARMOR
(keyword:ARMZID1)

When the leaf-like platform stops at the bottom of the long and spiraling column, you have the option to go back up to the top if you want. The party separates momentarily, so search for items as Zidane. There is a hidden chest at the bottom of the steps containing an Elixir, and another one behind the lower part of the column containing a Brigandine. Zidane may benefit greatly if you equip this armor on him right now.

When you examine the far-left edge of the area, something terrible rises from below, and then falls to annihilate the Iifa Tree's intruders.

SOUL CAGE BOSS FIGHT
(keyword:SCAGE1)
HP:
5700
AP:
 9
Weak Vs.:
 None
Steal Items:
 Brigandine, Magician Cloak, Oak Staff
Spoils:
 Elixir, Phoenix Pinion
If Vivi, Dagger, and Zidane know the Auto-Potion ability, then this long battle isn't too difficult to survive. Eiko can get by if she's learned the Auto-Regen ability. On subsequent rounds, she can summon Fenrir or cast Reflect on the others. If someone gets wiped out, Eiko should summon Phoenix to revive them, and cause some incredible damage to the enemy. The blue-haired sprite can be a real blessing in this fight. Avoid casting Fire spells against this Boss or else it will begin casting its Fire Blades attack. Keep this in mind when casting Phoenix.
If Zidane goes into Trance, use one of his more costly Dynes such as Solution 9 to end the battle rather abruptly. If that doesn't occur, he should steal and attack. Vivi should cast Bio each round and Dagger should summon Ramuh.
The Soulcage does have some fierce attacks. However, the party now has so many preventative measures that no one should get KO'd more than once the whole fight.
There is one attack called Mustard Bomb that kills a character after one round, but only if that character takes action. You can press the Triangle button to skip the afflicted character until "Heat" wears off. This creature can also summon Lv5 Death. But retaliate with Phoenix after such attacks as stated. To help from getting KO'd against the Mustard Bomb attack, equip the support ability "Body Temp.
