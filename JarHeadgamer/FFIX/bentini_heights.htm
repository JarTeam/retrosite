SHADOWS IN THE MOUNTAINS

Crossing the Bentini Heights area, you're very likely to come across Treno immediately. However, if you proceed past Treno for the time being and push through the expansive forest to the east, you can enter Quan's Dwelling and pick up some Ethers.

AP BOOST
(keyword: APUP1)
The Angelic Visitor. One of the creatures that seems to appear usually quite soon after entering the Heights is a little floating ghost with a halo. Avoid attacking this creature, or it will run in fear. Instead, command the first character to use some Ore on it. The creature responds in kind, and recommends you find someone named "Ladybug." Your party will receive 10 AP and a Hi-Potion!


QUAN'S DWELLING

This cavern is behind a long and dangerous stretch of forest to the southeast of Treno. You'll encounter some creatures called Mandragoras along the way, and you should fight them with everything you've got. This cave holds a few items and a few points of interest, and it's a great resting spot for parties seeking to level up in the surrounding area.

STELLAZIO COIN
(keyword: STELLA2)
Inside the first large cavern, open the box on the far left for an Ether. Go behind the large central column and climb down to the lower level. Down here is another Ether and a spring pond that restores HP and MP. While leveling up by fighting monsters outside, you can return here and refresh your party as often as you like. Your search isn't complete in this area until you find the Scorpio.
