HANGIN' OUT IN THE BIG TOWN

While a bewildered Dagger is prepared by the castle staff to become queen, Zidane drowns his sorrows at the pub. Eventually, his buddies give up looking for him and run into Vivi on the street. Now that you're controlling Vivi, there are a number of other things you can do in Alexandria.

Blank and Marcus are waiting for Vivi in the alley where Puck stole the ladder from Dante. You can walk past the two bandits, or you can speak to them. If you want to finish all of the mini-games, make sure you refuse their request to see Ruby's play. If you accept their request, it triggers a long sequence of events that take you out of Alexandria.

HIPPAUL'S BIG RACE

Hippaul and his mother stand near the entrance of town. When you speak to the mother, she asks you to race with Hippaul so that the pudgy boy will get some much-needed exercise. This mini-game is only available at this point of the game, and you can get some extremely rare cards and items for participating.

When the race begins, rapidly press the Circle and Square buttons to control Vivi's stride. A good tactic to follow is to place your controller on a table or flat surface and use your forefinger and middle finger to tap the buttons rapidly. You should barely lift your fingers from the buttons. Or, you can reconfigure the buttons according to your preference in the Config Menu, too.

At first, defeating the little guy is simple. However, Hippaul gains levels each time you race. This makes each race a little more difficult than the last. Honestly, this mini-game becomes extremely challenging after Hippaul reaches level 40. Naturally, that's when the rarest cards are up for grabs. For each 10 levels that Hippaul gains, his mother will give you a rare card. You can continue to race past level 80, up to level 99. However, you get nothing for your effort and it's tremendously stressful.

SHOPPING IN ALEXANDRIA

Since there's no performance at this time, the shops are open for business. Make sure you stop by Doug's Medicine Shop, the Alchemist's Synthesis Shop, and the Alexandria Weapon Supply.

In the Weapon Supply, the Nero brothers (Benero, Genero, and Zenero) play a rather simple gambling game with coins. You can play with them to your heart's content, but you can only win 25,600 Gil max. That's all the money they have!


THE NERO BROTHERS
You can only play the gambling game as Zidane; you cannot do so when playing as Vivi.

HOW ARE THE MOOGLES?

Stiltzkin may be hanging out with Kupo at the bell tower. In spite of the seemingly peaceful times, it's a good idea to continue purchasing items from him. You might have an overdue letter from Kuppo to deliver to Kupo. Also, remember when we said that you could find Hippaul's stash of cards by ringing the bell at the top of the tower? Well, if you didn't do it previously, you can now get two additional cards. In addition, you get the Ironite Card, Goblin Card, Fang Card, and the rare Shiva Card and Ramuh Card. You must plunder the stash now, because it's your last chance!


MOVING ALONG

Head to the left out of the square. Blank and Marcus are hanging out at the entrance to the mini-theater where Ruby now works. They ask Vivi to run interference while they sneak in, but there's no getting past a smart actress! In the meantime, Dagger is trying to get her act together before the coronation. Dr. Tot helps out by giving her an Opal, a Topaz, and an Amethyst. I'm sure the princess can use these items

Eventually, Baku and the rest of Tantalus get tired of Zidane moping around. When Vivi joins Zidane, you must guide the two toward the castle. There are some items to pick up along the way, so make sure you pick up the 2680 Gil at the mini-theater near the first table inside the door.
.

Head upward from the square to the place where a venetian-style boat takes travelers across the moat to the castle. After Freya and Amarant have a muscle-flexing contest, you can find a Phoenix Pinion and 3927 Gil on the left and right steps. Upon boarding the boat, you head to the castle.

Check behind the fountain to find a Lapis Lazuli, and head over to the left tower of the castle to find another Phoenix Pinion in a corner on the right. Going through the double doors leads to a chamber where you can ride a statue of Neptune to the harbor. Search the back right corner in this room for the Leo Stellazzio
.

After scouring the outside area, return to the castle's entrance, where Eiko is being forcibly ejected. The group then enters the castle and a long series of scenes commences. Eventually, the party decides to attend the card tournament currently underway in Treno.
