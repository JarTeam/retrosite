LIFE AMONG THE BRANCHES

Upon entering the Cleyra tree settlement, Freya will run off to confer with the king of Burmecia. One of the oracles offers to take you on a tour of Cleyra. This is a great way to get acquainted with the town. All of the branches are connected by similar rope bridges, and it's difficult to remember which bridge leads to which branch. You must become very familiar with the layout of Cleyra in a short amount of time.

The conclusion of the tour brings Zidane back to the front gate. Move to the right, into the area with the large sinkhole. There's a chest here with 970 Gil inside. Return to the front and head up the stairs. On the mid-level platform, search for a hard-to-find Phoenix Pinion. Then continue up either set of stairs to continue searching for items. Meanwhile, a series of ATEs will show you what kind of mischief your buddies are up to.
.

CLEYRA INN

There are several items to find in the large area that houses several buildings. Search the bushes in the lower-right corner, near the steps, to find some Ore. In front of the Inn is Dan, the soldier you saved in Burmecia. Although he doesn't treat Vivi very well, Dan will sell you weapons and armor. His inventory is detailed at the end of this section. Before entering the Inn, ascend the stairs to the right about halfway, and then search among the bushes for a Remedy.

You will find a depressed Vivi at the Inn. He is inconsolable, so proceed into the corner above him and reassure yourself with an Echo Screen. In the lower-right corner of the Inn, at the base of the stairs, you can find 1250 Gil. Go upstairs and search the nightstand by the bed for an Ether. Then talk to the moogle Mopli, who may have a letter for Zidane from Ruby.
.

WATERFALLS

Two Burmecian soldiers lament the fall of their kingdom near the life-giving falls. Also, Moon Maiden Claire is happy to discuss the magic that keeps the sandstorm in place around the settlement. Search the well nearby to find Thunder Gloves.

TOP OF THE FALLS

Star Maiden Nina sells potions and medicines. Her inventory is listed at the end of this section. It also seems that she's dropped a Phoenix Pinion near the ledge for you to pick up.

THE CLEYRA CATHEDRAL

At the very top of the tree is the Cathedral, wherein the citizens of Cleyra perform their magic ritual to maintain the whirling sandstorm protecting their home.

SPECIAL ITEMS
(keyword: ITMS3)
Search in the grass near the sign in the foreground for Gysahl Greens, and then enter the Cathedral. Check the ground just inside the entrance on the left for a Yellow Scarf. The guards barring access further into the chapel have a message from Freya to wait for her at the Inn.

MORE STUFF !
Before triggering the Antlion boss fight, visit the sand pit to the right of the town entrance to see an extra scene with Quina. This leads you to two previously inaccessible chests containing a Silk Robe and Magician Shoes.

PESTILENCE OF THE SETTLEMENT

After speaking to the guards at the cathedral and returning to the Inn, Burmecian Soldier Dan reports that a giant creature is attacking a child near the entrance of the settlement. Follow him down the stairs, and then proceed to the right into the area with the large sinkhole. The Antlion has arisen from the sands, and has Vivi's old friend Puck in its grasp.
.

If you equipped some characters with Desert Boots, then your chances are greatly improved since those party members will take less damage. Also, if more than one of your characters has learned or equipped the Auto-Potion ability, that will help as well.
When the Antlion performs its special Sandstorm attack, it will most likely reduce each character's HP to 5 or less. Freya should cast Reis's Wind after each Sandstorm attack, because the assault seems to negate the regeneration. This will help your fighters recuperate quickly from the attack. Also, if more than one of your characters has learned or equipped the Auto-Potion ability, that will help as well. Vivi should cast Blizzara each round, which should cause about 1000 HP per hit, especially if the Ice Staff is equipped. Quina should use an Ability s/he has learned to protect the group and attack, while Zidane should be relegated to stealing items and administering potions. Also, Quina's Pumpkin Head or White Wind abilities are useful to use after the Antlion unleashes its Sandstorm Attack.
Since the Antlion has a nasty counterattack if you use physical attacks, use magic and skills instead.

THE INVASION OF CLEYRA

ABILITIES & FREYA
(keyword: FREYA1)
After the Antlion battle, you'll control Freya for a short time. If Freya returns to the Cathedral, she receives an Emerald from the High Priest, which teaches the Dragon Knight the extremely useful White Draw ability. Then meet the party at the entrance and get prepared to fight before climbing back down the trunk.

The chamber with the sink holes is now dormant following the cessation of the sand storm. This is the first place where the heroes get attacked by Alexandrian Soldiers. The female warriors attack in pairs, but are extremely easy to overcome. Most of the time, they will run off rather than die. Try to kill as many as possible so that you receive plenty of Mythril Sword cards.

Proceed down the trunk, taking as few side paths as possible, until the party reaches the plank bridge and suspects a trap. Puck calls the entire party back to the village, where the real battle is currently underway!

At the town's entrance, you must protect the Shrine Maidens from a Black Mage and then a trio of Alexandrian Soldiers. This time, it's a no-holds barred fight where the enemies cast the powerful Blizzara and Fira spells over and over. Then more Black Mages appear, and the party retreats up the stairs. You can win these battles with ease by using straightforward fighting tactics.

To the right of the town's entrance, you can find the moogles Stiltzkin and Mopli. Stiltzkin will have another "value deal" available to you at a low cost, and Mopli will give you a letter intended for Serino. Make sure you save your game at this point.

Up the steps, Zidane encounters the Oracles, who are fleeing from enemies. A decision must be made to either go up the steps or flee to the right. The next couple of decisions you make determine whether or not some of the citizens of Cleyra survive. Each surviving townsperson will thank you by giving you an item. For example, if you tell the first two to "head up," Black Mages will appear and kill them. However, if you lead them to the right, they will live to reach the safety of the Cathedral.

Outside the Inn, Burmecian Soldier Dan's family is looking for him. Order them to "go left" and they will survive. In the meantime, you must fend off a combined Black Mage/Alexandrian Soldier party.

Zidane follows the family to the waterfall, where they must be commanded to follow the Oracles and cross the bridge to the right. Another vicious battle ensues, and then the survivors all meet at the overlook area.

After the group sorts things out inside the Cathedral, General Beatrix commits an act of thievery. Follow her out to the lobby, where all the survivors are gathered. Talk to each one to receive a prize for rescuing them. Mopli the moogle is also in the lobby, and will ask you to deliver a letter to Serino if he hasn't already done so.

Because you're about to face the warrior who decimated your group previously, this is a good time to save your game. Then prepare your party well and proceed outside to face off against the powerful general once again.

This battle is like a continuation of the last time. When you wear down half of Beatrix's HP, she will cast the Cure spell to restore about 650 HP during the battle.
Vivi should cast Slow on her immediately, and then take out around 640 HP with Blizzara each round. Quina should cast Mighty Guard if possible, and should be ready to use Phoenix Downs and Hi-Potions to get KO'd characters back into the battle quickly.
Zidane should again alternate between physical attacks and stealing, and Freya should cast Reis's Wind and perform Jumps. This battle will also end with Beatrix reducing everyone's HP to 1, after which she flees to Queen Brahne's airship.

STOWAWAYS

The heroes escape Cleyra in the nick of time, finding their way miraculously onto the Queen's hovering airship. Follow Beatrix to the forefront of the ship, where you'll overhear Brahne's savage plans for the Princess. It's up to you to save her!

As you head back to the launch pods, Serino the moogle will make an appearance. Your party should be back to full health, so don't waste a Tent. But you should have a letter for Serino from Mopli, and it won't hurt to save your game again. Then return to the pods and use them to reach Alexandria, where the others are being held captive.
