INTRODUCING THE QUS

The marsh is located north of the Dragon's Gate and south of Gizamaluke's Grotto. After crossing the planks into the marsh, you should run across the moogle tutors. You can review or catch up on any tutorials you skipped or didn't understand at this point.

Next, proceed into the high grass. You'll lose sight of Zidane completely, but just hold up on the directional button to get through. Regardless of wherever you enter the tall grass, you should enter a pond area where a jolly creature called a Qu is trying to catch frogs.

S/he isn't having much luck, so run around the area and press the X button to catch a frog. Then speak to the Qu to learn that its name is Quina. Another creature named Quale emerges, and escorts everyone back to its hut. Here you are offered the option of having Quina join your party. It's a definite benefit to have four party members at a time. Plus, Quina has the ability to "Eat" enemies and learn their special powers. This will come in quite handy in more than a few Boss fights.

Any time you return to the pond area, Quina will want to catch frogs. The number of frogs Quina catches is cumulative, so even if you catch some frogs and then leave the marsh, the number you caught before is remembered. The number of frogs Quina catches determines the items s/he receives from the marsh master.

QUINA & SIDE QUESTS
(keyword: QUFROG1)
After catching some frogs, you must leave the marsh and return later for more frogs to appear. This area is only one of many Qu Marshes scattered throughout the world, so visit and revisit them all to catch frogs with Quina. More on frog catching is detailed in the Side Quests chapter.

DON'T TAKE ALL THE FROGS!
Make sure you leave at least one male and one female frog, or it will take much longer to repopulate the pond!
