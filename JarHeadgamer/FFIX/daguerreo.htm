CITADEL OF THE WATER GOD

Daguerreo is located at the top of a mountain on an island in the Salvage Archipelago south of the Forgotten Continent. Pilot the Hilda Garde 3 to the largest landmass below the western frontier to find the entrance of the city. Land the airship on the plains, and move across the bridge to the large cave. The enemies that roam outside the mountain community are quite powerful.

Consider visiting this mountain dwelling before proceeding with the adventure. Daguerreo has a full stock of new and powerful weapons and armor, some of which you can only find here. You can also find some useful items and a few tasks to perform. Not only does Daguerreo boast great shopping, but the plains outside are an excellent area for leveling up characters and learning abilities.

LEVELING UP
The monsters around Daguerreo are tough, but after a dozen or so battles, you won't notice anymore. Since you can fully regain your strength just inside at the Library, Daguerreo is a very attractive area for leveling up your characters to a powerful status.

STELLAZZIO COIN COLLECTING
KEYWORD: STELLAX


Inside Daguerreo, begin your own self-guided tour by heading off the center platform down to the right. Search the sunken path for the Capricorn Stellazzio, and then continue into the passageway. A clever hydraulic-powered lift elevates you to the upper ledge. A moogle named Noggy always hangs out up here.

Directly across from the lift is the Synthesis Expert, who makes a whole slew of new weapons and items. You should browse through his inventory, which is listed at the end of this section. The first item of note is Zidane's Sargatanas, which requires a Gladius and a Zorlin Shape to make. If you don't have either of those items, you'll have to assist the Weapon Shop Owner in Daguerreo with a little supply problem he has. More on that further down.

THE MASTER TREASURE HUNTER

Near the Synthesis Expert, you'll encounter the mysterious Four-armed Man once more. This arrogant fellow assesses your Treasure Hunter Rank, which is a summary of all the treasures you've collected in the game up to this point, including those found in the Chocobo Hot and Cold side quest.

SPECIAL ITEM
KEYWORD: TRSHUNT1

If you've found a vast majority of the treasures, he will get scared and run out of the room. Follow him out to the entrance, where he will reveal his true identity and give you the Rank S Award. Until you achieve the Superior Rank, challenge this fellow to a game of cards. His deck includes most of the rare airship cards, and he's not what you would call a brilliant player.

DAGUERREO'S LIBRARY

RARE CARDS
KEYWORD: RARCRD2

The bookshelves at Daguerreo are legendary, containing a copy of every volume ever written on every subject imaginable. Near the right entrance is Engineer Zebolt from Lindblum, who is studying hydraulics. To his left are two people arguing; one is the Sales Clerk who runs the Medicine store, and her inventory is listed at the end of this section. Speak to the Young Man she's arguing with, and he'll ask you who's right. Agree with the Young Man's side every time, and he'll give you the rare Flare Card. Agree with the sales clerk every time, and she'll give you the rare Meteor Card.

Next to the Medicine shop is a strange block that Zidane can examine. It drops below, releasing an obstruction. Now speak with the scholar at the bookshelves on the left, who can't find "The Eidolon and I." Return to the books stacked to the right of the entrance, search them, and Zidane will spot the title. Return to the Scholar, and Zidane will point out that it's across the room.

This removes the Scholar from your path. Behind him is a hidden ladder that enables you to climb down to the mid level. There's a humble old man and the Librarian at the far-left side who will let you rest in a hammock for 100 Gil.

HIDDEN AREA
KEYWORD: NAMCRD3

After descending the hidden ladder to the mid level, move to the left and speak to the people perusing the bookshelves. If you have the Namingway Card, the Guy Doing Research is suddenly inspired to create. He will rename any of your characters, including Zidane. At the far left and far right sides of the mid level, there's an Elixir on each side. To get back to the second floor, return to the center of the midlevel and search where the "?" icon appears.

SUPPLY PROBLEMS IN DAGUERREO

The left exit from the library takes you to the Weaponsmith's Shop. However, he can't sell you anything because he's having trouble with his supplies. You must fix his problem before he can sell you anything. And believe me, you want to help this guy because he's got the good stuff!

To get his supplies flowing again, head back across the second floor library after speaking to the Weaponsmith. Go down to the first floor, and head straight across the entrance chamber to access the area beneath the Weaponsmith.

Examine the three lifts on the left wall. The levers on the top wall operate the lift, however, there's a malfunction that prevents the top lift from going all the way up to the second level. This is the heart of the Weaponsmith's problem.

Examine the levers. The Right lever raises and lowers the top lift, while the Left lever raises and lowers the bottom lift. Lower the Left lever until the bottom lift is at bottom floor level. Examine the bottom lift, and Zidane will spot a hole in the wall just above the platform. Now examine the staff leaning against bookshelves. Zidane will take it across the room and insert it into the hole. This keeps the bottom lift from raising.

Return to the levers and lower the Right lever until the top lift is at floor level. Move Zidane to it, and press the X button. He will get on and ride up to the second floor. Speak to the Weaponsmith and he will express his relief. He will immediately sell you equipment, and his inventory of amazing items appears at the end of this section.

BLESSING THE DRAGON GOD

SPECIAL ITEM
KEYWORD: AQAM3

After spending some time in Daguerreo, return to the entrance and examine the mosaic depicting the Water Dragon God. This causes something new to occur. For every 5 Ore that you place into the fountain, you receive one Aquamarine.


TRAINING IN DAGUERREO

As we mentioned previously, the mountain plain just outside town is one of the best areas in the game for leveling up. You'll encounter Grand Dragons and possibly a greedy monster called a Gimme Cat.

A NOT-SO-FRIENDLY CREATURE
The repugnant Gimme Cat creature pretends to be a Friendly Creature, like the ones detailed in the Side Quests section. However, take note that the combat background music is playing instead of the melodic Friendly Creature music!
The Gimme Cat will ask for Diamond, and if you give it one, it will flee. If you attempt to fight it off, the monster will retaliate with strong magic. The best thing to do is use Zidane's Flee ability to get away immediately. However, the Gimme Cat does give you a good amount of Gil if you defeat it.

Grand Dragons can also drain your characters, so escape if you have to and get some rest in the Daguerreo Library. However, a good strategy is to teach Quina the LV5 Death ability, which will kill a Grand Dragon instantly. Quina can learn this ability by eating a Whale Zombie on the south shore of the Lost Continent. If Quina is also equipped with the Millionaire ability, this is a good way to make a ton of Gil as well!
