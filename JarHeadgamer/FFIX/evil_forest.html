RISING FROM THE WRECKAGE

The Prima Vista crash-lands in a rather nasty region called Evil Forest. After Zidane rejoins the troupe, you can't re-enter the ship. Search through the cargo lying outside to find a Phoenix Down. Then prepare to search for the missing characters by equipping Zidane with the best armor pieces available. Make sure you judge the items you equip more by the abilities they enable your character to learn, rather than strength or defense.

ATE, KUPO!
Mosco the moogle introduces the concept of the "Active Time Event." This is a new feature in the FINAL FANTASY series, whereby you view scenes occurring in other locales involving characters that are not currently in your party. When the words "ACTIVE TIME EVENT" flash in the bottom-left corner of the screen, press the Select button to watch the event(s). If there is more than one ATE, you can view them in whatever order you want. Some ATEs are optional, as indicated by flashing blue words, but you must view some ATEs to advance the game.
THE DANGEROUS WOODS

When you're ready to search the Evil Forest, head through the hollow tree. Zidane will most likely encounter some Fangs and Goblins on his own, but you can eliminate them with 1-3 blows. Make sure you steal from each enemy to replenish and add to your inventory.

Not far into the woods, Zidane finds Vivi and Steiner watching as Garnet gets attacked by some strange forest monster. The two rivals will team up to take on the wretched plant.

This battle occurs in two fights. First up is Zidane and Steiner against the unholy plant. Zidane will immediately go into Trance at the start of the battle, enabling him to cast the Free Energy ability, which inflicts almost 250 HP damage per strike. The Prison Cage will Absorb around 20 HP from the Princess each round, so it's important to hit it with everything you've got. Don't waste your time trying to steal from Prison Cage at this point, because it has nothing that you can steal. Also, if Garnet's HP gets too low, make sure you use a Potion on her.
Just when you thought the coast was clear, the Prison Cage returns, sans Princess, and grabs Vivi! The little black mage will cast Fire each round, causing 80 HP damage. This makes the evil plant mad, and it absorbs 25-30 HP from Vivi each turn. Also, make sure you attempt to steal an item or two from the Boss; there are definitely some worthy items available. Still, with Steiner attacking and with Vivi casting Fire repeatedly, you should be able to steal good items from the plant and then finish it off just before it completely drains your little buddy. Keep a close eye on Vivi's HP too; if it gets too low, use a Potion.

QUITTING THE CREW

Back onboard the wrecked Prima Vista, Zidane decides to go looking for the Princess. However, Baku won't let Zidane go that easily. After the captain is finished lecturing, open the treasure chest on the ship's bridge to find Bronze Gloves.

As you go downstairs, you can view some ATEs. Afterward, Blank emerges from Vivi's room. Go in and speak to the mage, and then search the room for an Ether and 116 Gil on the top bunk bed. Outside Vivi's room, Zidane must decide whether to quit Tantalus and go searching for Garnet. Whichever option you choose, Zidane will search for her, but it's fun to see how the two different choices affect the dialogue.

Continue searching through the Prima Vista, opening treasure chests to find a Rubber Helm, a Potion, a Wrist, and an Ether. Go down into the hold into the meeting room, where Baku will accept Zidane's dismissal. However, Zidane must face Baku in one-on-one combat before he can leave!

Baku is not intent on killing Zidane, but the game will end if you don't watch your HP and take Potions when needed. Baku will trip and fall during half of his attacks. But don't think him a fool, because Baku is really trying to get Zidane to steal some helpful items from him. If you can steal the Iron Sword for Steiner to use, your party will have it much easier in the Boss fight to come! So alternate attacks with attempts at thievery. After obtaining the Iron Sword, continue to attack until Baku calls it quits.

AN UNEASY ALLIANCE

Now that Zidane is free to go, return to the room in which Steiner is being held. Steiner joins your party, albeit hesitantly. Before moving on to talk to Vivi about joining, return to Steiner's holding cell and open the chest for an Ether. Then make sure you fully equip Steiner with good items that will let him learn some abilities, plus the Iron Sword if you were able to steal it from Baku.

When Vivi joins, equip the mage with a Leather Wrist and a Silk Shirt if you have them. By doing so, Vivi can use Blizzard and Thunder magic in addition to Fire. On your way out of the hold, Blank will stop Zidane with some harsh words. But ultimately, he gives Zidane Blank's Medicine to use on the Princess when you rescue her. After the dialogue, return to the room in which Blank is standing and search for a Leather Hat.

Outside the ship, Cinna will sell Zidane medicines and Tents if desired. After that, save your game with the moogle before traveling through the forest.

Eventually, the party enters a small clearing where a crystal-clear pond will fully restore HP and MP. It's important to drink from small ponds like this all throughout the game so that the party can continue without using too many Tents. Monty the moogle is hiding in a hollow tree stump nearby, so give him the letter from Kupo and save your game if desired.

(keyword:PLNTBR1)

916

 Fire
Steal Items:
 Iron Helm, Eye Drops
Spoils:
 Phoenix Down, Potion
Not far after the save point is the cavern of Plant Brain, who holds the unconscious Princess Garnet captive. Steiner should attack with Vivi's help, using all of his MP to perform the Fire Sword magic attack. Vivi can also cast Fire on his turns, but it's best to use the black mage to administer Potions and Eye Drops as necessary. Zidane should alternate between stealing and attacking. Soon enough, Blank appears and joins in the fray. Blank's attacks are more powerful, so Zidane should only steal and administer Potions from there on. When the message "Doesn't have anything" appears, Zidane and Blank should only attack from that point forward.
Early on, Plant Brain performs the Pollen attack, which causes Darkness status to all characters. Zidane should administer Eye Drops to himself immediately, so that he can continue to accurately pummel the plant with physical attacks. The plant will attempt to kill Steiner with Thunder attacks, so have Vivi use Potions on ol' "Rusty" every other round to keep the big guy in shape.

A DESPERATE ESCAPE

While the heroes attempt to flee the forest, they are forced to encounter the dangerous Plant Spiders once or maybe twice. There isn't much to steal from these creatures except Potions, so everyone should attack at full force and use Fire magic if possible.

Blank saves the rest of the party, and throws the Continental Map to Zidane. This item will allow the party to mark important locations on the World Map, so that travel in the large areas beyond will be easier. You can view the Continental Map while the party is on the World Map by pressing the Select button.
