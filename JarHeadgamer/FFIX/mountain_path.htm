THE LAST OF THE SUMMONERS

As the group heads west of Conde Petie through the mountains, a little purple-haired person is chased out of town by the dwarves. The party stumbles across the tiny girl, who had been stealing food from Conde Petie to survive.

Her name is Eiko, and she permanently joins the party. You'll notice right away that she knows absolutely no abilities, but she can summon like Dagger. Eiko can learn Fenrir to start, and then she will pick up the awesome Phoenix summon from a Phoenix Pinion, if you have any in stock. Make sure you equip Eiko with armor and a bracelet right away, so that she's well protected and begins to learn abilities.

Climb up the left slope, and use the vine to reach to the upper ledge. Open the chest to receive a Remedy, and head to the left until you reach a statue. Take the Blue Stone out of the icon and return to the starting point. Head to the right until you see another vine to climb. On this lower ledge, head to the left and get the Tent out of the chest. Another statue nearby holds the Red Stone.

Return to the lower path and continue to the right. Up a sharp slope you'll meet the moogle Suzuna, whom you may hold a letter for. You may also encounter Stiltzkin, who offers to sell some more items. Move down to the left, climb up the ladder, and cross the giant tree root to the right.

You've certainly been wondering about the origin of the tree roots and their meaning. Now the answer lies before you, as Zidane spots the massive Iifa Tree far off in the distance. However, as you continue across the root to the end, you realize that getting there may be a tough battle.


HILGIGARS BOSS FIGHT
(keyword:HILGG1)
HP:
8106
AP:
 9
Weak Vs.:
 Thunder
Steal Items:
 Fairy Flute, Mythril Fork, Phoenix Down
Spoils:
 Elixir, Tent
The giant casts Earthquake during this battle, causing a severe HP loss to anyone standing on the ground. You can prevent this by having Dagger or Eiko cast Float on the entire party. However, Dagger can also summon Ramuh, because the thunder god will take down about 1500 HP from the monster.
Vivi should be extremely helpful as well, because he can cast Bio for another 1600 HP damage per strike. Relegate Eiko to summoning and curing any party members. You need to dispose of this Boss quickly, or it will cast Curaga to regain 2200 HP. This may occur after a half-dozen rounds of battle.

EIKO'S HOME

After the Boss fight, Eiko invites everyone to her house in the nearby town of Madain Sari. First, examine the statue in the fork in the path to get the Yellow Stone. Then proceed along the right path. Curve off onto the bottom path and swing down to a spot with an Ether and a statue containing a Green Stone.

EIKO & SPECIAL ITEM
(keyword:CSTONE1)
Return to where Suzuna stands guard, and take the rightmost of the three paths. In this area under the tree root, you'll find a different statue to stick all four of the stones in. You should receive a Moonstone for your efforts, which will teach Dagger the Shell ability if she doesn't have it already. Also, equipping the Moonstone will make Carbuncle cast "Pearl Light," which casts Shell on all party members.


Now go back to the fork and continue to the right until you leave the Mountain Path area.
