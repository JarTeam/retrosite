MUSTERING THE TROOPS

The beautiful city of Alexandria is besieged by the black horrors unleashed by Kuja. Dagger must quickly and precisely give out orders to the Knights of Pluto. If you don't know what you're doing, then not all of the townspeople will survive, and you'll only receive an Ether. However, if you know which orders to give to the soldiers, then you'll receive the rare Angel Earrings.

The answer harks back to the very beginning of the game, when Captain Steiner had to give orders to all of the Knights of Pluto. If you spoke to Breireicht twice on the stairs, he rattled off a list of the knights and their personalities. Judging by their interests and abilities, you should know which order to give to which two knights. Refer to the answers that follow exactly to receive the better prize.

You can tell that you're giving orders properly when the knights respond enthusiastically. If you mess up, the knights will be unsure of their orders.

SAVE THE QUEEN

Beatrix and Steiner run down Alexandria's main street, protecting the townsfolk from the evil Mist monsters invading the city. You will have a battle in each area, and your party will consist only of Beatrix and Steiner. Considering Beatrix's abilities, that's not necessarily a bad thing.

Equip Steiner with an Ice Brand and the Add Status ability. This may allow you to freeze a Mistodon, so that Beatrix can crush it on the next turn. Otherwise, Beatrix and Steiner should be able to attack a Mistodon once each and survive to the next battle.

When fighting two Mistodons at once, have Beatrix perform Climhazzard to bring them both down. You may only need one more blow to finish them off. Between each battle, use Hi-Potions and Ethers to keep both warriors energized.

Before this series of battles ends, you may want to consider exchanging the items that Beatrix is wearing for some of the more beneficial fare in your inventory. You can use the Cross Helm, Thunder Gloves, Plate Mail, and Yellow Scarf that she's wearing to the benefit of Steiner or Freya later. However, Beatrix's Save the Queen sword is a weapon only she can equip, plus only she can use it.

CALL OF EIDOLON

Dagger feels a strange energy emanating from the entire castle and senses a calling from upstairs. Any route she attempts to take that isn't correct becomes blocked by a wall of invisible force, so it's really easy to determine where to go.

Lead Dagger up the circular steps to the third floor, and go through the double doors toward the Queen's chambers. Veer to the left, and go up a series of circling staircases. Each one becomes sealed off by the swords of two knight statues, preventing Dagger from turning back.

At the top, the castle transforms into a new shape. Lead Dagger up the stairs until the game takes over.

EVACUATE ALEXANDRIA

Zidane and the party arrive just in time to rescue Dagger and Eiko. Your fellow warriors are Vivi, Freya, and Amarant. Before following Dagger's path to the top of the castle, you may want to play the hero and clear everyone out. There's even an optional Boss to fight, if you think you're prepared!

OPTIONAL BOSS FIGHT!
(keyword: OPTBOSS)
You can fight a really tricky optional Boss at this point, if you think you have the patience and skill. However, you should visit the moogle in the Knight's Guardhouse on the second floor and save your game before attempting this. You don't want to replay the last half hour over again!
To find the Tantarian, go to the lower left bookcase in the left side library and examine the outer corner. An ominous voice will attempt to ward you off. Challenge it, and you'll be swept into a very long and tricky battle.

This optional battle is also available when attempting to rescue Dagger before the Red Rose arrives. However, this fight is much easier to win at this point in the game with this party.
Equip Amarant and Freya with items from which they haven't learned the abilities from yet, since winning this battle will instantly bring their abilities up to snuff.
Make sure you equip everyone with Antibody and some form of HP regeneration, such as Auto-Regen or Auto-Potion. Equip Amarant with the Poison Knuckles, and make sure that Freya knows or is capable of using the Lancer ability. Also, make sure Zidane is equipped with the Bandit ability. Tantarian has some really awesome items to snatch.
Tantarian first appears as a giant floating book, but the real monster is inside. While in book form, the Tantarian can perform devastating attacks that will take a nice chunck out of your party's hit points. However, since the monster inside doesn't have the same abilities, it's important to get that book open!
There's a good chance that Amarant's Spare Change ability will open the book instantly. If he throws 200 Gil or less, then you're all set. This causes the book to open, and when it does you'll get to fight the monster inside. However, Amarant might throw more than 200 Gil, so be prepared to open the book another way.
Start off with physical attacks only. Each time a character strikes the book, it opens to a certain page. You must strike the book for just the right amount of HP, so that the book opens up somewhere between pages 150-200. If you cause more than 200 HP of damage, then the page opening process starts over.
To avoid going over the correct amount of HP, command each character to strike, and then wait for them to execute their attack before letting the next character perform. Zidane and Amarant will cause the most damage, so have Freya protect the group by casting Reis's Wind.
When the book finally opens, it's important not to strike the book again physically. This means that you must use only magic and skills to attack. The monster will only defend itself with the Poison spell, so if everyone is equipped with Antibody, you'll be fine. Each round, have Zidane perform Thievery while Freya performs Lancer. Amarant should perform Spare Change, but Vivi can cast Bio over and over. If Zidane Trances, have him perform Grand Lethal two or three times to speed things up.
If the book closes again, you must get it back open as soon as possible. If not, it will perform Paper Storm or Edge on every turn until your party is dead. The book may close again briefly, even if you don't strike it physically. Don't let this throw you, as it's just trying to confuse you. Use another physical attack to open up the book. The Tantarian grants 3146 EXP to all party members, plus 4472 Gil.

If you're not interested in the optional Boss, then follow Dagger's trail up to the top of the castle. The scene then shifts back to Lindblum, where everyone is recuperating from the attack.
