ECHOES IN THE DARKNESS

Kuja has dragged Eiko into a truly dark and foreboding underworld. The enemies here strike with a lot of fire-based magic. Make sure Vivi is in your party, along with Steiner. This duo enables you to retaliate with Ice and Water magic, simplifying your battles. Dagger is still not the best person to bring along. Her magic and attacks continue to fail half the time. Also, prepare everyone with the Body Temp and Antibody abilities.

QUINA & NEW ABILITIES
KEYWORD: QINA5
EAT SOME MORE!
Quina will have a blast in here, eating enemies and learning great abilities and spells. From the Grenades, Quina can learn Mustard Bomb, from the red Vepals s/he can learn Lava Gun, and the Worm Hydra yields the Bad Breath attack.


From the entry point, head into the house on the left. Search amongst the rubble for 9693 Gil and a bulletin board with an important message about a well. On the top floor, you can pick up an Ether. Continue through the left exit and climb down the ladder to the lower platform. Search here for a Red Hat, and then return to the entrance and continue to the right.

Proceed through the next dilapidated hut, and then find a rope to slide down. Before descending, run around in front of the hole and jump up to the platform on the right side. Follow it back into a cavern. Open the treasure chest to get a Golden Hairpin, which teaches the Regen ability to Dagger and Eiko.

In the top corner is another bulletin board that explains how to operate a lever somewhere. Now slide down the rope into the chasm. This deposits you near a well; the lever that controls it is on the left-hand side. There are some items to get on this level before you pull the lever and continue to descend.
.

Head to the right first. This is where Moolan the moogle stands vigilant. Give him the letter from Mogrika, which contains more clues about Mognet. He then asks you to deliver a letter to Mogtaka in return. In the bottom-right corner, you'll find a Wing Edge. Save your game, and then continue to the right.

Another dilapidated house has a staircase to climb on the far right. There's another message at the top in the corner with a clue about the lever. Continue up the steps and head out the top-left exit. Follow this platform to the end, where you'll find another set of Gaia Gear armor. As you return to the well area, a couple of Red Dragons burst through the roof and attack. You can dispose of them using Water magic, and if Quina eats one, s/he can learn the awesome Twister spell.

It's time to explore the area to the left of the well. Go past the cave opening into the crumbling house. Search to the right inside the condemned building to find a Demon's Mail, and then check the wall to the left to find another message. This one indicates that the true solution to the well is to pull the lever three times.

Exit the left house and go through the opening in the wall. Up the stairs is Mogtaka, for whom you have a letter. Mogtaka also runs a Mogshop. Stock up on Hi-Potions and then continue up the stairs. On the top level, another Red Dragon bursts through the wall and attacks. After defeating it, grab the Elixir in a chest hidden in the shadows.
GREED OF THE DARK SPAWN

Finally, return to the well and pull down the lever three times to lower the rope. Climb down into the well bottom, where another set of Red Dragons break through the wall, creating an exit. Defeat the dragons and step through the dust. The party can see that the jester twins, Zorn and Thorn, are attempting to extract the Eidolons from Eiko.

The scene shifts down to the floor for a quick Boss fight between Eiko and the jesters. However, the battle immediately ends when Eiko summons Madeen.

Afterwards, Eiko receives the Ribbon, which enables her to perform that summon from this point forward. Unfortunately, this power isn't available in the next battle.

 MELTIGEMINI

MELTIGEMINI BOSS FIGHT TIPS
KEYWORD: MELT1
HP:
24,348
AP:
 11
Weak Vs.:
 Fire, Holy
Steal Items:
 Demon's Vest, Golden Hairpin, Vaccine
Spoils:
 Vaccine (x2)
The previous battle gives Kuja some insight into the Trance process. He escapes, and Vivi chases after him. This means that Eiko will replace him in your current party, unless he wasn't in the party to begin with.
After the first round, the creature performs an attack that gives everyone a virus. You'll spend the next round administering Vaccines to everyone; otherwise, you won't receive any AP. Since the battle is actually somewhat easy, don't kill the creature until everyone's viral status is cured.
Most of the monster's attacks involve poisoning your characters, so equip everyone with Antibody. You may have to compensate for Eiko, since you really don't have a chance to equip her properly before the fight begins.
To kill Meltigemini easily, cast Mini on it. If Eiko or Dagger isn't successful at doing this, use Quina's Bad Breath ability. Once in this state, one Critical hit will kill Meltigemini.
This battle also yields a good amount of Gil, especially if Quina is equipped with the Millionaire ability. After finding Lady Hilda in a nearby room, the scene shifts back to Lindblum.
