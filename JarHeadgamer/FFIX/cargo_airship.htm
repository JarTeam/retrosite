IT'S GONNA BE A BUMPY RIDE


After resting in Dali, you'll be in better shape to make the journey to Lindblum. Return to the airfield and board the vessel. After a few laughs and a few shudders, lead Zidane up to the ladder at the front of the engine room, and go up to the deck to find Steiner lamenting on the deck.

When you enter the ship's bridge, Zidane takes over and turns the ship around. Looks like the rest of this adventure should be smooth sailing, but that's hardly the case. As the Cargo Ship heads toward Lindblum, the ship is tracked by the most powerful of the black mages.

This battle is somehow a bit easier than the previous. For starters, Vivi goes into Trance right at the start of the fight, enabling him to cast two magic spells in succession. Have Steiner use Swd Mag attacks as often as possible.
Waltz No.3's one main defense seems to be its ability to float in the air. Once it rises off the deck, regular physical attacks miss every time. This puts Zidane at something of a disadvantage throughout the entire battle. Therefore, have him steal and cure the others with Potions when needed.

No.3 breaks off the attack rather than perishes, so steal all the items you can from it because there are no spoils afterward. Afterwards, the Cargo Ship floats onward directly to Lindblum.
