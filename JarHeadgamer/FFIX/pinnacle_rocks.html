THE SPECTER OF RAMUH

As Dagger, Vivi, and Zidane recover from the crash-landing of the gargant, the Eidolon of Ramuh appears and challenges the group to a trial. If you assemble his story correctly, you'll receive a Peridot, which enables Dagger to summon Ramuh in battle.

The group is led by Dagger throughout this event. Following the encounter with Ramuh, Dagger walks into an area where Monty the moogle waits to record your game. Make sure you give him the letter that you received from Mosh. After doing so, return to the first area, where Ramuh will appear again and recite the 'Hero' part of the story. Now return to where Monty sits and head up the top branch. Ramuh appears in your path and recites the 'Beginning' part.
.

Continue across the branch, and then head to the right across a bridge. Halfway down the lower branch, a "?" appears. Jump off the branch to reach the treasure chest below and claim the Mythril Armlet. Moving to the left causes Ramuh to appear once more, reciting the 'Silence' part of the story. Go around the ramping branch, head toward the chest, and Ramuh should appear in the corner. This time he recites 'Cooperation,' and will allow you to get The Ogre from the chest.

Head down from that area to the place just below Monty's perch, where a chest contains a Mythril Vest. Now run under the window set into the tree, and Ramuh should appear for the last time just behind it. He now recites 'Human,' the last piece of the story.

RAMUH & DAGGER
(keyword:RAMH1)

To finish the event, you must find Ramuh at the exit from Pinnacle Rocks near the location where you jumped off the branch. He now expects Dagger to assemble the narrative in a coherent manner. While there are five pieces to the story, only four are needed to complete the tale. The first three parts must be selected according to chronology, and they are:

'Beginning'

'Cooperation'

'Silence'

Whether you choose 'Hero' or 'Human' next depends entirely upon your personal point of view in life. Ramuh then reviews the story as you assembled it and asks if you want to correct it. He will point out any errors in assembling the first three parts and let you try again if need be. When you're satisfied, Ramuh will examine your choice of ending. No matter which ending you choose, Ramuh will bestow his abilities upon Dagger in the form of a Peridot to be equipped on Dagger as an Add-on.
