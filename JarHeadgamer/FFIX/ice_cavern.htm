WINTER WONDERLAND

The group is impressed by the icy interiors of the cavern. However, you must be on guard nevertheless, for great dangers lurk herein. First, hop onto the area to the left and snag the Tent. Then continue upward into an area with a fork in the path.

VIVI & THE ICY WALLS
(keyword: ICECAV1)
Then go up the natural steps to the right and search the icy wall. Vivi will use his Fire spell (at no MP cost) to remove a thick layer of ice blocking a treasure chest, which contains an Ether. Now you know to look for these walls throughout your trip through the Ice Cavern. Before proceeding up the steps, cross under the embankment to get a Potion out of the visible chest.

MONSTERS OF THE ICE
All of the creatures in the Ice Cavern are extremely sensitive to fire. Use Vivi's Fire spell and Steiner's Fire SWD MAG to quicken battles before your enemies gain the upper hand. The monsters have powerful abilities that drastically reduce your characters' HP and can even put them to sleep. They won't hesitate to use these abilities if you're not swift and merciless in your battles.

In the next chamber, move along the left wall until you reach the top, where you can find another iced-over chest containing an Elixir. Then cross to the bottom of the chamber and up the fallen stalactite to the chest holding a Potion. Go back down to the bottom of the stalactite and examine it. Vivi will blast it free with his magic, unveiling the Mage Masher in the chest. Equip this weapon on Zidane IMMEDIATELY, even if you haven't learned the abilities from his previous weapon.

Follow the path to the next area, where Vivi will uncover an entire path blocked by ice. Quickly run down to the chest on the ledge to get a Leather Wrist, and then backtrack to the other side of the chamber for a Phoenix Down.

As you continue onward, the group encounters a fork in the road and has no clue of which way to go. By all means, head to the left first. By taking this path, you come across a frozen moogle named Mois. Upon releasing the moogle, he asks that you deliver a letter to Gumo for him. A brief tutorial on Status Effects, Elemental Properties, and the Card Game ensues. Save your game and use a Tent if needed, because a difficult trial is up ahead.

Return to the fork in the path and head to the right, where the party gets fiercely assaulted by freezing winds. The group is then mysteriously put to sleep one by one, but only Zidane wakes up. Follow the sound of the ringing bell to reach the source of the sudden blizzard at the top of the slope.

BLACK WALTZ NO. 1 & SEALION BOSS FIGHT
(keyword: BWALTZ1)
HP:
 229/472
AP:
 5
Weak Vs:
 Fire (both), Ice (Black Waltz No.1)
Steal Items:
 Black Waltz No.1: Silk Shirt, Remedy; Sealion: Ether, Mythril Dagger
Spoils:
 Hi-Potion, Phoenix Down, Fang Card/Skeleton Card
The insidious Black Waltz immediately summons the titanic Sealion into battle. Both enemies cast Fire and Blizzard spells at Zidane, so take Potions whenever his HP gets around 100 or so. If Zidane dies, the Game Over screen appears as your only reward.
Stealing during this battle is a nightmare. You should only attempt to steal if you have a lot of Potions to use in between each attempt. Focus your attacks on the Black Waltz first, or it will heal the Sealion by casting Blizzard spells on it.
Your best hope is that Zidane goes into Trance soon. With the Mage Masher equipped, he can perform Tidal Flame, which takes almost 375 HP from both enemies. After doing so, you should only need to hit the Sealion one or two more times to end the battle victoriously.

After the battle, Zidane will backtrack and gather the troops. Then return to the falls and scale the steep cliffs to reach the exit from the Ice Cavern.
