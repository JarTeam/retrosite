BIO

JUMPING AND DRAGON KNIGHT SKILLS

While her normal physical attack is mediocre in power, use the Jump command to make Freya leap high into the air, where she gathers enough power to perform her devastating Spear attack. While in the air, Freya cannot be attacked or inflicted with negative or positive status effects. When Freya Trances, command her to Jump, and she will remain in the air, showering all enemies with a hail of spears!

Freya learns powerful and extremely helpful Dragon Knight abilities by equipping various lances and spears. However, since these are considered skills and not magic, she can still use these abilities in places where magic is negated!
