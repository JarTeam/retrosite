THE SANDY TREEHOUSE

The story finally returns to Zidane's party, where they were last seen languishing in Burmecia. Upon leaving the fallen kingdom, head west to the whirling sandstorm protecting Cleyra. Most of the citizens from Burmecia have fled the area, so you can visit there now. However, the trip won't be easy as you attempt to climb up the massive trunk full of flooding sand, swirling traps, and dangerous monsters. Reorganize your party so that everyone has the best equipment possible.

KUPO FOR KUPO NUTS!!
(keyword: KUPNUT1)
Remember to return to Gizamaluke's Grotto and give Moguta a Kupo Nut. Each time you do, the couple will have another baby moogle running about. Moguta will gladly trade good items for Kupo Nuts.

Upon entering the sandstorm, the characters uncover the path leading up the trunk. At the entrance, pull the lever to the right to open the seal. Just inside, head to the right for a Phoenix Down, and then continue up the path to a sand-filled room. There are some partially hidden chests in here, so don't leave until you've collected an Ice Staff and Magician's Shoes. The main reason to get these items now is because you're about to flood this room with sand, and you won't be able to get the items afterward.
.

TRUNK ENEMY NOTES
You're bound to encounter the Dragonflies and the Sand Golems while navigating the Trunk. You can defeat the Dragonflies if Vivi casts Blizzard on them. To eliminate a Sand Golem, you must only attack the core. The red center is extremely weak against Ice spells, so equip the Ice Staff on Vivi.

Run up the slope outside the tree, all the way to the top to find an Ether. Now put Zidane's hand into the hole to the bottom right and trigger the switch; this causes sand to pour into the room below. This cuts off the lower access, so return to the outside of the tree and climb the vines into the large ornate entrance.

Cross the sand-flooded room to the opposite hole, and search for a Needle Fork at the base of a tall slope. On the midlevel is a Tent and the moogle called Monev. You may have a letter for Monev from Atla. Monev will give you another Kupo Nut for delivery of the letter!

Continue up the rise and take the left stairs in the next area to find a Flame Staff. Then cross a plank bridge into a cavern with three caves at the back.

HIDDEN ITEMS
(keyword: ITMS1)
First, search for the Desert Boots hidden behind the center column and equip them on a party member. There's a chest to the far right that contains a Remedy. Going through the right tunnel takes you back to the center of the cavern. Search in the left ventricle (where the "!" mark appears on the picture) to find a Mythril Vest. This armor teaches characters the Auto-Potion ability, which should be learned by all. Now head through the middle cave of the cavern.

Heading through the cave in the very back leads to a chest containing Mythril Gloves. Press the switch on the pulley at the end of this path to make another chest available in another area. Upon doing this, return to the main cavern and exit through the far-left passage.

Take the Potion from the next chest on the path, and veer up the right branch to get an Elixir from the chest. Return to the path and continue to the left, where Zidane nearly runs into a whirling sink hole. If he gets caught in one of these holes, continuously press the X button to avoid being sucked in.

Before continuing to the left, pick up the Hi-Potion and 900 Gil from the chest. At the end of the path, there's a ladder leading up to the settlement. Grab the Gysahl Greens before you go.

UNDERSTANDING THE SINK HOLES
When you're caught in a sink hole mashing the X button to get out, press the D-pad or analog stick in the direction opposite the target area where you want to land. This ensures that you'll end up where you desire upon leaping from the sink hole. However, if you don't release the analog stick as soon as Zidane jumps out, you may go right back in the hole! Also, use the D-pad and walk around the sink holes, don't run! This increases your chances of avoiding the whirling traps.
