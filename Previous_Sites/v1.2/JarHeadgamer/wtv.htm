Possible Wii unbrick?

I may have found a Wii unbrick. I had a bricked RVL-101 Wii (Family Edition) that seemed to brick out of nowhere. When I hit power nothing would appear on the screen. What I did was hold Sync, Reset, Power and Eject at the same time and that worked! If you have a bricked Wii try that and it may come back to life.
