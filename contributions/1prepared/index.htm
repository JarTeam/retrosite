If your computer is 2000 or below, you might have serious problems using this guide

You must have a discord and a github account, heres the guide to do so

Creating a Discord account

You first go to discord's website and regrister an account (steps below)

In the website, scroll to the bottom

You will find a sign up now button, click on it

You will be prompted for your email, username and a unique password. Sign up with your details and click on continue.

And you're done!

Creating a Github Account

First go to Github's website

Click on Sign up

Insert your details and click on "Select a plan" after verifying

Select the invididual free plan (choose another if you plan on using github later on with paid features)

And fill out your details (you can skip this step)

And verify your email (inbox) there will be a link and you would click on it

Clicking on it will redirect you to the first time setup (you're done!)
